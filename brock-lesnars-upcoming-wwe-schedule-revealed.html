Brock Lesnars Upcoming WWE Schedule Revealed

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

WWE is going through a lot of changes, and that includes how they market talents. The company is now releasing big talent’s schedules, and Brock Lesnar’s upcoming itinerary was next in line.

WWE previously released Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins’ schedules. That also gave Rollins a chance to throw some shade at Reigns’ part-time schedule.

Brock Lesnar has an ultimate part-time schedule in WWE. He only works a few times a year, and 2023 is no different. Although he’s worked multiple premium live events in a row for WWE, it doesn’t appear that he will be around until the July 3rd episode of RAW after Money in the Bank.

You can check out Brock Lesnar’s schedule below, which WWE released today. Only time will tell if the Beast Incarnate surprises fans with an additional appearance.

July 3 from the CFG Bank Arena inn Baltimore (post-Money In the Bank show)

July 17 from the State Farm Arena in Atlanta

July 31 from the Toyota Center in Houston (SummerSlam go-home show)

It has been mentioned that Lesnar vs Cody Rhodes III is scheduled to take place at WWE SummerSlam in Detroit, which falls on Saturday, August 5th. As of right now, Lesnar is not being promoted for WWE Money In the Bank on July 1st.

It appears that Brock Lesnar will be around for SummerSlam, and he will face Cody Rhodes. Their match will likely be a unique one, and a dog collar match is reportedly already off the table.
