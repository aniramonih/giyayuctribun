Khloe Kardashian, Kendall Jenner Sport Skintight Swimsuits on Yacht

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Too yacht to handle! Khloe Karashian and Kendall Jenner went booty-to-booty in skintight bathing suits while aboard a boat in St. Barts during their family getaway last week, and Us Weekly has exclusive photos from their sexy sea escape.

The photos, which were taken on Aug. 19, feature the reality star siblings in body-hugging swimsuits that show off every curve. The shots include a svelte Khloe, 31, in a Baywatch-inspired red bathing suit with an open back that accentuates her enviable derriere. Up top, the blonde Kardashian sister wears a black “Compton” baseball cap, and a pair of large hoop earrings.

Her model half-sibling, meanwhile, shows off her runway-ready physique in a similar black number that features a deep open back. Caitlyn Jenner‘s daughter, 19, models her lean stems and toned back as she and her sister pose together at the bow of the boat.

Other photos show them tanning and lounging on the boat with white glass goblets in hand.

The latest pics may look familiar to Kardashian-Jenner family enthusiasts, after similar snaps were shared by the sisters themselves just last Wednesday. Both Kendall and Khloe posted the bootylicious swimsuit photos via Instagram, posing on the boat and holding up peace signs.
