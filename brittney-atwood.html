Brittney Atwood Is A Mother Of Two Children; Wedding Officiated With Husband, Roman On July 27, 2018

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 9, 2024

Brittney Atwood is an American YouTube star widely known as the wife of Roman Atwood, who is considered as the YouTube phenomenal personality. 

Mrs. Atwood was born on 16th February 1991, in Ohio, United States of America. Born to her American parents, she is of White ethnicity and holds an American nationality.

Marital Life With Roman Atwood

Mother of two like Sheila Falconer, Brittney is living happily with her husband for more than a year. She and her spouse, Roman Atwood exchanged their wedding vows on the 27th of July 2018.

As per the couple, they started dating a long time before tying the knot and now live with their three children. The first one is a son Noah from Roman's previous marriage with Shanna Riley.

Whereas, rest of the two children, Kane Atwood (born on 23rd October 2011), and Cora Atwood (born in 2017), are of Brittney and Roman.

Became famous after appearing in Roman Atwood's Vlog

On the other hand, Brittney's partner Roman Atwood is a very famous YouTube star with more than 10 million subscribers. His channel is mostly recognized for hidden camera pranks.
