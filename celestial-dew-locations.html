All Celestial Dew Locations in Elden Ring

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 9, 2024

Here, we will tell you how to find all the Celestial Dew in Elden Ring.

First Published April 7, 2023, 04:59

FromSoftware games often hold many secrets in their games. The developers of these games will often have a treasure chest hidden away waiting to be discovered, and powerful weapons or even entire systems are hidden away for players to discover. Elden Ring is no different and there is a lot to discover in the game.

There are a lot of different items in Elden Ring that are hidden throughout the map. Some are more useful than others while there are some items that have a limit on how many there are. Celestial Dew is a pretty precious resource in Elden Ring and is hard to come by. Here, we will go over where you can find Celestial Dew in Elden Ring.

Celestial Dew Locations In Elden Ring

In Elden Ring, there are a lot of different areas that you can explore. This means that you might have to put in some leg work to get the Celestial Dew that is hidden away in the game. After all, Celestial Dew is a pretty precious resource in the game. Here is the list of locations where you can find Celestial Dew in Elden Ring:

  • Ainsel River - sold by Hermit Merchant
  • Caria Manor-  sold by Pidia
  • Uhl Palace Ruins - on a corpse and another on the ground
  • Nokron Eternal City - on a corpse
  • Night's Sacred Ground - on a corpse
  • Nokstella Eternal City - on a corpse
  • Nokestella Eternal City Site of Grace - go north, and it is next to some snails
  • Raya Lucaria Academy - Inner side behind a locked gate
  • Nights Sacred Ground - on a corpse
  • Erdtree Sanctuary - on a corpse on the upper floor

As you can see above, there are only a couple of Celestial Dew in Elden Ring. That is why it is important to know where they are and to not burn through them. It is very difficult to get any more once you have ended up using all of them.

What Does The Celestial Dew Do In Elden Ring?

In Elden Ring, you can attack essentially anyone or anything. This includes friendly NPCs that are in the game. After attacking a friendly NPC enough in Elden Ring, they will always attack you on sight, which can lock you out of certain questlines that are in the game. There is a way to reverse this.

The first thing you need to do is go to the Church of Vows in Eastern Liurnia. If you see a giant turtle, you are in the right place. In the middle of the Church, there is a statue surrounded by water. You will ask for absolution by offering one of the Celestial Dew. Once you do that, all of the NPCs that are trying to attack you will stop.
