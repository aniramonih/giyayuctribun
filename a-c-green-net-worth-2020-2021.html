Early Life & Biography

Green was born on 4th October 1963. The initials “A.C.” in his name stand for his father’s mother, Amanda, and his father’s father, Chester. Green converted to Christianity while he was in High School. He was raised in Portland, Oregon, U.S.A. He graduated from Benson Polytechnic High School and then attended the Oregon State University. His name was in the Oregon Sports Hall of Fame in 2003 due to his impressive basketball talent. He was a very obedient student. He was an excellent basketball player and a student. He was known in his school for not missing a class in his school life.
