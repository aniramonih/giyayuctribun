Billy Bob on Angelina Jolie: 'I never felt good enough'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 12, 2024

Billy Bob Thornton has revealed he always felt inferior to former wife Angelina Jolie - and opened up about how hard he found it trying to live up to her high standards. "I never felt good enough for her," the 61-year-old actor told GQ magazine about his fifth wife. "she was always going off to meet with the U.N. people or the president or the adoption agencies."

The Bad Santa 2 star and the 41-year-old actress met on the set of the 1999 film Pushing Tin and were married a year later in 2000. The pair, who got tattoos of each other's names and wore vials of each other's blood around their necks, eventually split in 2003, three months after Angelina adopted her first son Maddox from Cambodia.
