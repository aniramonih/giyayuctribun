Columbus, OH man, Matt Majesky is missing since Oct. 24

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

Columbus, Ohio – A sense of deep concern blankets the community as 43-year-old Matthew Majesky, a Columbus resident, has been reported missing since Tuesday, October 24. Matt left his home for work but never arrived, leaving his family frantic and worried. Despite their efforts, there has been no contact from him, and his phone appears to be switched off.

Matt Majesky was last seen driving a Dark Brown Honda CR-V, believed to be a 2015 model, with a license plate starting with “GWH.” He was dressed in a black t-shirt, blue jeans, and black New Balance shoes. Matt is described as having red hair, hazel eyes, a red beard, standing at 5’9″ and weighing 270 pounds.

The sudden disappearance of Matt Majesky has left his family and friends in a state of distress, desperately seeking any information about his whereabouts. Law enforcement agencies, led by Detective Grocki of the Columbus Police Department, are urging anyone who may have seen him or knows anything about his disappearance to come forward immediately.

Friends, neighbors, and concerned citizens are joining the search efforts, distributing flyers and sharing information on social media platforms in the hopes of locating Matt. The family is making a heartfelt appeal to the public, asking for assistance in bringing their loved one back home.

Detective Grocki can be reached at (614) 645-4017. Any piece of information, no matter how small it may seem, could prove to be crucial in locating Matt Majesky and reuniting him with his family. As the community unites in this search, there is a collective hope for his safe return, and everyone is urged to remain vigilant and supportive during this challenging time.
