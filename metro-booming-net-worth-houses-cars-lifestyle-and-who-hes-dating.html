Metro Booming Net Worth, Houses, Cars, Lifestyle, and Who hes Dating 2022

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 23, 2024

What is Tyler Wayne’s Net Worth? Who? Well, you probably know him by his stage name, Metro Boomin. He’s a 28-year-old American record producer, DJ, music executive, and rapper. Let’s find out Metro Boomin Net Worth in 2022. Also, know more about Tyler Wayne’s Beautiful House and fast cars.

Metro Boomin’s Net Worth in 2022 is $8 Million.

Metro Boomin has a net worth of $8 million as of 2022 and as for his source of income, let’s say music.

Leland developed a passion for music at an early age. He played bass guitar in middle school and started to make beats when he was 13 using the FruityLoops software.

His initial goal was to produce beats he could rap over but producing music won him over.

While attending Parkway North High School, Leland began to reach out to established rappers and send beat samples to potential music placement companies.

It finally paid off cause while in the 11th grade, he started making frequent trips to Atlanta for collaborations with artists he met online. His mother was very supportive and drove him to this meeting, an eight-hour drive, FYI.

One of the initial artists Leland did a collabo with was 1017’s Tay Don. And this created an opportunity for him to collabo with other 1017 label stars like OJ Da Juiceman and Gucci Mane.

OJ was the first known artist to rap over Leland’s beats.

Leland soon finished high school and enrolled at Morehouse College to study Business Management. He, however, put a hold on school after he realized it would be too tasking to balance music and schoolwork.

Metro Boomin

His zeal, strategic networking, and talent in music production finally got him working with successful artists including Wiz Khalifa, Big Sean, Offset, 21 Savage, Nicki Minaj, Future, Drake, Meek Mill, Young Thug, Dj Khaled, Rich Homie Quan, Travis Scott, Young Jeezy, Ludacris among others.

Albums he has helped them compose or has produced like Honest, What a Time To Be Alive, EVOL, Stoney, Issa Album, among other singles that have gone platinum (a million-plus sales) and gold (500k+ sales).

Moreover, he has also produced his music. In 2014 he released his mixtape titled 19 & Boomin, and in 2016, an Ep titled Savage Mode, whose two singles, X and No Heart, have each sold over a million copies.

Boomin has also produced these albums: Perfect Timing (2017), Double or Nothing (2017), and Without Warning (2017), whose 2018 ‘Rick Flair Drip’ single has so sold over 500,000 copies.

No wonder Metro Boomin is the new boss in town, with an $8 million net worth as of 2022. He has a wealthy clientele and charges around $150k for studio time, about $40,000-50,000 for concerts, and he also gets a payday from downloads. For instance, Drake’s Jumpman earned him $400,000 in 2016.

Metro Boomin is bound to surpass the $8 million net worth at this rate.
