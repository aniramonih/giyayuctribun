Meet American singer, Carly Ritter daughter of late actor, John Ritter

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

Carly Ritter is an American actress and singer who has surprised the world through her incredible talents. She has starred in the movies like Monsieur Balloons, Slice, and Artbound. Further, she is also widely recognized as the daughter of the late American actor and comedian, John Ritter. 

Los Angeles native, Carly has been a fan of folk music since her childhood, and eventually, she has written and sang numerous songs. Some of her songs are It is Love, Princess of the Prairie, Storms on the Ocean, Silver Dagger. All of the songs are from the self-titled album Carly Ritter which was released through Vanguard Records.

Married life of Carly Ritter

The beautiful celebrity daughter, Carly is a married woman. She exchanged her wedding vows with her longtime boyfriend, Tim Cape. The two started dating one another in 2010 and has been together since then. After dating for five years the duo accepted one another as husband and wife back on 7th September 2016.

As of now, the pair are happily married for more than four years. There is no news regarding pair having any external marital till now. Despite having a busy schedule Mr. and Mrs. Cape still find time for one another and are often seen spending the holiday together. 

Parents love life

Carly is the daughter of huge celebrity parents. Her father, John Ritter is one of the legendary actor and comedian who has won almost every award with his incredible talents. Similarly her mother, Nancy Morgan is also an American actress who has acted in numerous movies and TV series till now.

The pair met one another for the first time back in 1975 and started dating. After years of dating, they tied the wedding knots in 1977, However, after nineteen years of wonderful married life, the pair got divorced in 1996. On 18th September 1999, John married actress Amy Yasbeck at the Murphy Theatre in Ohio.

Relationship with Siblings

Carly is not only the child of the late actor, John. She grew up with her two brothers Jason Ritter, born on 17th February 1980, and Tyler Ritter, born on 31st January 1985. Both of her brothers are actors who have appeared in numerous movies and TV series. 

Furthermore, she has a half-sibling, Stella Ritter who was born on 11th September 1998. The four children of John are very much close to one another. They usually visit one another on various occasions and spends quality time together. 

Net worth 

Carly's estimated net worth is around $500,000 as of 2020. She is a good musician and actress and has also involved in entrepreneurship. Her estimated annual income is somewhere between $100,000 to $150,000. Currently, she is enjoying a happy and prosperous living with her husband. 

Similarly, speaking about her father's wealth, he was the owner of the $20 million fortune. However, he died on 11 September 2003 at the age of fifty-four in Burbank, California. Millions of fans from all around the world paid a huge tribute to the death of the legend and his family.

Bio

American singer, Carly Ritter was born on 1st March 1982. Her full name is Carly Constance Ritter.

She graduated from Crossroads School which is located in Santa Monica. Later she earned Phi Beta Kappa from Vassar College. Further, she is highly involved in social works.
