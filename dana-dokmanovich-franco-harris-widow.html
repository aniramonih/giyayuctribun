Dana Dokmanovich, Franco Harris widow

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Dana Dokmanovich is the wife of the late NFL Hall of Fame Franco Harris. Harris primarily played for the Pittsburgh Steelers, with one season as a player for the Seattle Seahawks.

Dana and Harris shared a long marital relationship before his passing. She often accompanied her husband to public events during his lifetime.

However, Dokmanovich has kept most details of her personal life private.

To learn more about the widow of the legendary footballer, including her background, parents, children, and current activities, read on.

Dana Dokmanovich

Dana Dokmanovich was the wife of the late Franco Harris, the former NFL football player renowned for his iconic role in “The Immaculate Reception” during his time with the Pittsburgh Steelers and the Seattle Seahawks.

The couple enjoyed many years of marriage before Franco’s passing.

While they made appearances together at various public events, Dana chose to keep most aspects of her personal life private, resulting in limited information about her.

Franco Harris, born on March 7, 1950, in Fort Dix, New Jersey, was a Steelers legend and a four-time Super Bowl champion.

His famous “Immaculate Reception” play remains one of football’s most unforgettable moments.

How Dana Met Franco Harris

Franco Harris married Dana Dokmanovich after a period of dating, although the exact wedding date isn’t publicly available.

Harris, known for his privacy regarding his personal life, didn’t divulge much about his dating history or wedding details.
