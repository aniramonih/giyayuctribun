Roger Stone

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

Birth Name: Roger Joseph Stone, Jr.

Place of Birth: Norwalk, Connecticut, U.S.

Date of Birth: August 27, 1952

Ethnicity:
*50% French-Canadian
*25% Italian
*25% Hungarian

Roger Stone is an American political consultant, lobbyist, author, strategist, and conspiracy theorist. He was convicted on seven felony counts in 2019, and later pardoned by President Donald Trump.

Roger is the son of Gloria Rose (Corbo) and Roger J. Stone. He was raised Catholic. He later took on “Jason” as his middle name.

His family’s surname was originally French, Laroche. La roche translates to “the rock” or “the stone.”

Roger’s paternal grandfather was Noris J. Stone (the son of Jerry/Jeremiah Laroche/Stone and Angelina/Angeline G. Graveline). Norris was born in Connecticut. Roger’s grandfather Jerry was the son of Joseph Laroche/Stone and Delima Berthiaume, who were born in New York, of French-Canadian descent. Angelina was French-Canadian, the daughter of Frank Graveline and Angelina/Angeline Cote.

Roger’s paternal grandmother was Marie Flosie/Flouise Maud Almina Edwina Favreau (the daughter of Joseph Vital Ephrem/Ephraim/Ephriem Favreau and Mary Pigeon). Edwina was born in Massachusetts, of French-Canadian descent. Joseph was the son of Vital Favreau and Seraphine Jasmin, who were born in Québec. Roger’s great-grandmother Mary was the daughter of Damasse Pigeon and Marie Hermine/Armine/Anna Patelle/Petelle/Patel, who were also born in Canada.

Roger’s maternal grandfather was Anthony Corbo (the son of Rocco Corbo and Catharine Sibilio). Anthony was born in New Jersey or Connecticut, to Italian parents. Catharine was the daughter of Lorenzo Sibilio and Giovanna.

Roger’s maternal grandmother was Grace C. Mezzo/Mego (the daughter of Andrew Mezzo/Mego and Rose Evansco). Grace was born in Connecticut, to Hungarian parents. Rose was the daughter of John Evansco.

While Roger is sometimes referred to as being Jewish, this is not accurate. He is not of Jewish heritage.
