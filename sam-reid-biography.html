Sam Reid Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Who is Sam Reid?

Sam Reid is a famous Australian actor, best recognized for his role as Earl of Essex in Anonymous (2011) and as John Davinier in Belle (2013).

He is also known for his incredible role in the movie The Railway Man (2013). He is the older brother of the famous actor, Rupert Reid.

What is the birth sign of Sam Reid?

Sam Reid was born on February 19, 1987, in New South Wales Australia. His nationality is Australian and his ethnicity is Irish.

He belonged to a middle-class Australian family. His father was a cattle farmer in New South Wales. He has a younger brother Rupert Reid, an actor.

Sam Reid completed his school education at a private school, Cranbrook School, in Sydney. Later, he moved to New York City for further studies. Sam longed to become an actor since his childhood.

After spending a few times in New York City, he shifted to London to study acting and attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art and graduated in 2010.

Sam Reid was the best student in his class and he was awarded Star Student of his graduating class.

While he was at the academy, he attended several auditions and also performed in theaters.

During his final semester at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, he auditioned for the film Anonymous, and he grabbed the role of Earl of Essex.

Sam Reid: Professional Life, Career

Sam Reid has been active in the industry since 2007. He began his acting career in early 2007, appearing as Marty Arent in an episode of All Saints (2007).

After getting the role of Earl of Essex in the movie Anonymous, he began preparing for his role at the start of 2010, and the movie premiered in 2011. The same year, he appeared as Young Harry in Spooks.

Sam Reid was cast as Brian Lomax in the TV film Endeavour (2012). He also appeared as John Davinier in the period drama Belle (2013).

His role as Younger Finally in The Railway Man (2013) made him more popular and he began getting more roles in movies.

Since then, Sam Reid has appeared in several movies including Serena (2014), The Second Coming (2015), and  Despite the Falling Snow (2016).

In early 2017, he was cast for the role of DI Len Bradfield in the TV series Prime Suspect 1973, and he is currently working with the series.
