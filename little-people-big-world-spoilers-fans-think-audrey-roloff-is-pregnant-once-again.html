Fans Think Audrey Roloff Is Pregnant Once Again

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Little People, Big World spoilers reveal that fans have started to think that Audrey Roloff is having another baby. It all happened in one of her new posts that she shared on Instagram this week.

Audrey shared a video of herself with her children and she was wearing a bikini. Her stomach was sticking out a little bit in the video, and of course, her followers began to speculate immediately.

Audrey has decided to respond to the comments about her being pregnant and she was not happy with the way they made the assumption about a possible fourth pregnancy.

Little People, Big World Spoilers – The Post Gets Followers Excited For Baby Four

As soon as Audrey’s die-hard fans saw that her belly was sticking out a little bit, they started in on how they thought that she was pregnant once again.

Most of them would like to see her have another baby and she has told fans that she isn’t finished having children yet. However, it doesn’t look like she is pregnant again.

Audrey went to address her fans on social media, telling them, “Really guys? Don’t we know not to ask this question? But no, I’m not pregnant. Guess I should have chosen this pic/angle instead, but Rad looked cuter in the other one.” In the photo she shared with her response, her belly was flat as ever.

She added, “I feel constantly misunderstood, especially in this space. I’m sure you have too. And I Think it can be tempting for all of us to come online and try to set the record straight… try to explain and defend your thoughts, opinions, beliefs, dreams and lifestyle to anyone who seems disapproving in any way.”

Little People, Big World Updates – Audrey Roloff And Life Now

As of right now, Audrey will continue to stand up for her beliefs and her life. According to her, she is not pregnant, but she has not thrown out the idea of more children.

She does get a lot of judgment and backlash on her social media pages, but it looks as if most of it doesn’t bother her one bit.

She continues to share her essential oil business, the lives of her children, and all of the dates that she and her husband, Jeremy Roloff go on.

Audrey is one of a kind when it comes to what she believes, but she does have a devout follower that love to keep up with her on Instagram. We will continue to keep an eye on her to see if there are any other pregnancy rumors.
