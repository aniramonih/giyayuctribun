Brandon Hatmaker and his ex-wife Jen divorced for what reason?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Brandon Hatmaker and his wife, Jen Hatmaker, made headlines when they announced their divorce on social media.

Jen’s Instagram post on September 7, 2020, in which she shared a photograph with the title ‘Pray for us,’ was accompanied by a lengthy caption addressing her and Brandon’s breakup.

“First and foremost, I want to thank you for your kindness throughout the last two months.”

Jen thanked her Instagram followers before delving into the implications of her and Brandon’s divorce.

She described the parting as ‘unexpected,’ despite her difficulty putting it into words.

Jen also expressed her shock, anguish, and broken heart over her breakup with Brandon.

She also asked the public to grant the family some privacy during this difficult period following the couple’s divorce, writing,

“I’m going away with the kids for a little bit, and I’d like to ask for your kindness…and some breathing room. Please respect our children’s and extended family’s privacy.”

However, aside from addressing her current situation following their divorce, the message did not reveal anything about the basis for their divorce.

Bandon filed for divorce on August 21, according to a source. The divorce papers, however, did not provide the reason for their divorce.

The couple rose to prominence as a power couple who created Austin New Church and published books. However, they are best recognized for their 2016 stint on My Big Family Renovation, an HGTV show.

The couple married in 1993 and have five children from their marriage. Three of their children are biological, while the other two are adopted.

Although the reason for their divorce is unknown, rumors suggest that it was due to Brandon’s extramarital involvement with Rachel Hollis.

Brandon Hatmaker and Rachel Hollis rumor

Rachel is the CEO of Chic Media and the founder of TheChicSite, a lifestyle website.

She had previously featured on Jen’s podcast, For the Love of New Beginnings.

It’s unclear how the word of Brandon and Rachel’s affair began, but it didn’t take long for it to spread like wildfire.

While Brandon was allegedly having an affair, Rachel’s divorce from her husband, Dave Hollis, in 2020 added fuel to the story when many suspected the affair played a role in Rachel’s divorce.

Rachel discussed her divorce on Instagram, revealing some details about her difficult decision to leave her marriage. However, she did not specify the cause of their breakup.

People were eager to tie Rachel’s divorce with Brandon’s unsuccessful marriage even though she had not said why the divorce occurred.

While it is unknown whether the report is true or a fake, the reason behind Brandon and his ex-divorce wife is also unknown because they do not want to divulge their issues.

Although Brandon and Jen’s relationship has ended, their parental obligations to their children will always keep them connected in some way.
