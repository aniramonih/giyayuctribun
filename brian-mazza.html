Brian Mazza is a famous entrepreneur from the United States. Brian Mazza is widely known for appearing on the cover of Men’s Health two times. Moreover, Mazza founded and runs High-Performance Lifestyle Training.

Early Life and Childhood

Brian Mazza was born in New York, United States on 8th November 1984. He is 39 years old under the zodiac sign Scorpio. Mazza holds an American nationality and follows the Christian religion. Besides that, he has not revealed any info on his family history including his mother and father.

Brian Mazza – Education

Speaking of his educational background, He began attending the University of Rhode Island in 2002. Mazza obtained his degree in communications and the arts with honors from the university in 2006. He received a full scholarship to the university to study while playing soccer.

Career and Professional Life

Talking about his career, In May 2007, Mazza began his career in the hospitality industry with Paige Hospitality Group. He currently serves as the hospitality group’s president.

He was a founding partner of the New York City restaurant The Ainsworth in 2009. The eatery gave the all-American sports bar and restaurants a classic touch.

He spread the bar to numerous places throughout the year, including Chelsea, Kansas City, Fidi, East Village, Nashville, and many others.

Further, Mazza founded the men’s clothing business Windsor Custom in 2010. He established MAZZA SPORT in January 2016. It is a brand of athletic apparel owned by BMAZZFIT. He also happens to be the brand’s owner.

He was the cover star of Men’s Health in 2017. After that, he was hired by numerous publications, including Men’s Fitness, Esquire, GQ, The New York Times, and many more. He was on the cover of Men’s Health once more in 2009.

Further, He started the High-Performance Lifestyle Training in 2019. He also serves as the training facility’s CEO. He currently represents Lululemon for Guys as their brand ambassador.
