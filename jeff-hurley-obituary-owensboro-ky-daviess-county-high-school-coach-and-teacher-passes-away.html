Jeff Hurley Obituary: Owensboro KY Daviess County High School coach and teacher passes away

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

The Owensboro, Kentucky community is grieving the loss of Jeff Hurley, a beloved teacher and coach who left an indelible mark on the lives of many. In this article, we pay tribute to the life of Jeff Hurley and the legacy he leaves behind.

Jeff Hurley was not just an ordinary teacher and coach; he was a remarkable individual who possessed a unique ability to provide comfort and encouragement to those in need. His impact extended far beyond the classroom and the sports field. Jeff was a devoted husband to his wife, Tammy Hamilton Hurley, and a loving father, proving that dedication and passion could lead to greatness in both personal and professional life. He embodied humility, radiated joy, and brought about positive changes in the lives of everyone fortunate enough to know him.

For an impressive 25 years, Jeff dedicated his career to Daviess County High School (DCHS). During his tenure, he took on various coaching roles, demonstrating his versatility and commitment to the school’s athletic programs. He served as an assistant coach for football, girls’ basketball, and baseball and even held the prestigious position of head baseball coach. In 2018, Jeff Hurley retired after dedicating a total of 28 years to public school education, leaving behind an enduring legacy in his community.

The passing of Jeff Hurley was a profound shock to the Owensboro community. His unexpected departure left a void that will be deeply felt by all who had the privilege of knowing him. At this time, the exact cause of his passing remains unknown, and we eagerly await further details. A heartfelt message from one of Jeff’s former students, Tammy Howe White, captured the profound sadness experienced by many.

“Incredibly sad after hearing the tragic news about Coach Jeff Hurley. What an amazing coach, teacher, and friend. He was one of my first teachers at Maceo Elementary and my last PE teacher at DCHS. I will forever remember his huge heart and love for his family and students. Prayers for his family during this difficult time.” – Wrote by Tammy Howe White

The family of Jeff Hurley has requested privacy and time to cope with their loss and begin the healing process. They will share details of Jeff’s obituary and funeral arrangements at a later time. This will allow friends, acquaintances, and well-wishers to pay their respects and offer support to the family during this challenging period.

Jeff Hurley’s legacy will continue to shine brightly through the lives he touched as a teacher, coach, and friend. His unwavering dedication, boundless compassion, and positive influence will be remembered and cherished by the Owensboro community for years to come.
