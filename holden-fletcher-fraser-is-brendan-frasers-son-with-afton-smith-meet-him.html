Holden Fletcher Fraser is Brendan Frasers Son with Afton Smith Meet Him

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Holden Fletcher Fraser is the second son of Brendan Fraser, the Canadian-American actor, and his ex-wife, Afton Smith. He is a 19 year-old American model born on August 16, 2004 and signed under Marilyn Agency.

Coupled with his passion for modeling, Holden Fletcher Fraser may also have an interest in following his father’s career path as he is very supportive and proud of his his career in Hollywood. During the 2023 Oscar awards, Holden and his younger brother graced the occasion to support their father.

How Old Is Holden Fletcher Fraser?

Holden Fletcher Fraser is 19 year-old and was born in 2004 to the family of actors Brendan Fraser and Afton Smith both of the George of the Jungle fame. At the time of his birth, his father, Brendan Fraser, had already started his career as an actor and had already achieved some level of success.

Although Brendan Fraser was already famous by the time he had Holden Fletcher, he did not reveal so much about the birth of his son at that time. As such details about Holden’s childhood are not available to the media.

Brendan Fraser’s Son Is A Model

According to the information on Holden Fletcher Fraser’s Instagram account, he is a model with the Marilyn Agency but not much is known about his achievements as a model. On Instagram, Holden has over 2,000 followers despite having just a couple of posts. Judging from his pose in the only picture he has on IG, you can easily tell that he is a model.

Aside from modeling, Holden is also a college student. According to available reports, he is a student of N.Y.C but exact details on what he is studying is not available to the public. However, there are reports that he may be studying a course related to acting or filming, because his father was invited to their filming class to have a live question and answer.

Holden also attended the New York screening for ‘The Whale’ with his father and younger brother.

Holden Fletcher Was At The 2023 Oscars Award

During the 2023 Oscars, fans were moved as they saw Holden Fletcher and his brother attend the award event to support their father who was an award nominee. While speaking to E-News, Holden and his brother, Leland Francis Fraser, expressed how proud they were of their father.

They also mentioned that despite the fact that their father was an Oscar awards nominee, he still had time to crack dad jokes with them.

Holden Fletcher’s Father Is A Famous Actor

Holden Fletcher Fraser’s father, Brendan Fraser, is a Canadian-American actor who is famous for featuring in movies like George of the Jungle, The Mummy trilogy, Journey to the Center of the Earth, The Quiet American, Crash, and Bedazzled.

His most recent work in Hollywood was the role of Charlie, an overweight teacher who was trying to mend his relationship with his daughter, Ellie – with Sadie Sink playing the role of Ellie.

Brendan Fraser’s contribution to the entertainment industry has not completely gone unnoticed. He has been honored with awards like Best Actor at the Seattle International Film Festival and Best Guest Actor in a Comedy Series at Online Film & Television Association Award.

Holden Fletcher’s Mother Is Also An Actress and Author

Afton Smith is an actress and writer. According to her IMBD profile, she has featured in movies like Reality Bite, Fried Green Tomatoes, George of the Jungle, and Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star.

As an author, Holden Fletcher Fraser’s mom has also written and published books like Hollywood Picks the Classics: A Guide for the Beginner & the Aficionado, Point to Happy: For Children on the Autism Spectrum, and The Tiger: Class of January 1922.

Holden Fletcher Fraser’s Parents Were Married For About 9 Years

As at the time when Holden was born, his parents, Afton Smith and Brendan Fraser, were married. The couple met in the 4th of July 1993 at Winona Ryder’s house. Winona Ryder was a mutual friend who had invited them for a barbecue. After their first meeting, they both got the chance to feature in George of the Jungle in 1998 where Brendan played the main role as George and Afton was Ursula’s friend.

Fast forward to 27th of September 1998, the love birds exchanged marital vows. As a couple, they welcomed three children, including Holden Fletcher Fraser. After almost 10 years of marriage, they announced they were going their separate ways but would still remain friends and co-parent their children.

His Parents Had A Complicated Divorce

According to reports, Bredan Fraser and Afton Smith were maintaining a good relationship despite being divorced, but their court cases did not seem to correlate with the statement. As part of the divorce settlement, Fraser was asked to pay an alimony of $50,000 to Afton Smith for 10 years or until she gets married to someone else. He was also to pay child support of $25,000.

After paying the sum for a few years, in 2011, Fraser went back to court to contest the alimony, requesting that it should be reduced because he does not earn enough to meet up with the stipulated sum. On her part, Afton Smith accused Fraser of fraud. According to her, at the time of the divorce, he hid his earnings from Extraordinary Measures and Furry Vengeance. The sum of both film contracts worth up to $9 million.

In the end, the court ruled out both Fraser’s request to reduce the alimony and Afton’s accusation of fraud. But despite their back and front at the court, both Afton Smith and Brendan Fraser are actively involved in the lives of their sons.

Holden Fletcher Fraser Has Two Brothers

Holden Fletcher Fraser is the middle child of Brendan Fraser and Afton Smith. He has an elder brother named Griffin Arthur Fletcher and a younger brother known as Leland Fletcher.

Griffin Arthur Fletcher

Holden’s older brother, Griffin Arthur Fletcher, was born on September 17, 2002. Brendan Fraser once revealed that Griffin is on the autism spectrum. It’s no news that people with autism may have some issues socializing, but according to Fraser, Griffin has a way of getting to people such that when he is around them they have little more compassion and they do not put themselves first.

Besides. Griffin is a big lad. He is 6 feet 5 inches tall and has huge body parts. Fraser credits Griffin’s physique for helping him fit into the role of Charlie for ‘The Whale’.

Leland Francis Fletcher

Holden Fletcher’s younger brother, Leland Francis Fletcher, is the last child of the family and was born on May 2, 2006. There are reports that Leland shares similar interest in modeling with Holden.

He is a musician and is said to be a member of a music band called The Alligators. You can find him on Instagram with the username leland_fletcher27. On Instagram he shares pictures of himself and his music band.
