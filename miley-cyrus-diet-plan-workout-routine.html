Miley Cyrus Workout Routine

She does a variety of workouts to stay is shape like dancing, cycling, etc. But, she gives more stress to Pilates workout. The 5 ft 5 inch tall celebrity can be seen attending Mari Winsor’s Winsor Pilates in LA. She attends it almost 6 days a week for an hour per day. Pilates is great for strengthening your abdominals and toning your stomach. It makes the deep abs muscles strong too. So, this means your food sucking muscles also get strengthened. It is an overall body sculptor which maintains the balance between your inner and outer body and thus you feel better.

You may perform the moderately difficult 5 min exercise for developing core strength. The exercise is called “open leg balance” and an exercise mat is need for this.

  • Sit tall on a flat surface with your sit bones (the bones which you feel, when you sat on a firm surface are the sit bones).
  • Bring the feet towards the body. The knees should be open and the feet must be together. Then, move your arms and it should be inside of the legs. Now, hold your ankles.
  • By giving a little more attention to lifting the lower abs, pull the abs in.
  • Post that, extend one of the legs and stabilize there. Then , extend other leg too. Make sure, you don’t make your movements while sitting on the tail bone.
  • Balance there and do hold for a count of five.
  • Now, carefully (with control), fold both the legs, one by one.
  • Check your body posture again and repeat this process multiple times (3 to 4 times is sufficient).

Miley Cyrus Diet Plan

Gluten and lactose free diet is the secret behind her such a lean figure. She also tweeted regarding this. She wants people to try this diet for a week atleast and the changes, which you observe in the mental, physical health and the skin are amazing. This will make people to stay with this type of diet.

Gluten is a protein and it is clear from the name itself that the diet excludes the gluten protein (found in wheat, barley, rye, triticale). This kind of diet is suggested for treating celiac disease.

As Miley Cyrus goes through this diet, there must be some foods, which she indulges into and some, which she avoids.

According to mayoclinic.com, foods which are ALLOWED to eat –

  • Fresh eggs
  • Fresh meats, fish, poultry
  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Beans, seeds, nuts in their original and unprocessed nature
  • Most of the dairy products

Some grains can also be made a part of this diet –

  • Amaranth
  • Quinoa
  • Rice
  • Soy
  • Flax
  • Corn
  • Millet
  • Arrowroot
  • Buckwheat
  • Sorghum
  • Tapioca
  • Teff

AVOID the following foods –

  • Avoid wheat and its products like bulgur, durum flour, kamut, farina, spelt, graham flour, semolina.
  • Triticale (a cross between wheat and rye)
  • Rye
  • Barley and also the products made from barley like malt, malt vinegar, etc.

You are allowed to try the foods below, if there are labeled as gluten-free –

  • Bread
  • Candies
  • Beer
  • Cakes
  • Pies
  • Cookies
  • Pastas
  • Oats (as they are also mixed with wheat during growing and processing stages)
  • Crackers
  • French Fries
  • Veggies in sauce
  • Soups
  • Matzo
  • Salad dressings
  • Snacks like potato and tortilla chips
  • Sauces including soy sauce
  • Gravy
