Is Lindy Booth Married To Jeff Wadlow? Know Her Personal Life Details

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 5, 2024

Canadian actress Lindy Booth's personal life has been the subject of talk in the entertainment industry since she has opted to reveal anything about it to date. Her secrecy has, therefore, made it hard for many people, including her fans, to know if she ever managed a relationship or married life with either boyfriend, partner, or husband.

The actress's decision to stay mum on her love life has also brought several speculations of her being secretly married.

The assumptions of her secret marriage got, even more, stronger when a marriage registration website, TheKnot.com, posted that she is married to American film director Jeff Wadlow.

The website shows that Booth tied knots with Wadlow on October 4, 2014.

Marriage registry site suggests that Lindy Booth is married to Jeff Wadlow. (Photo: registry.theknot.com) 

Likewise, the Film Journal outline reports that both Booth and Wadlow worked together for a long time on sets of several movies, leading them to fall in love with each other.

After dating for a while, the film director eventually proposed to the actress, somewhere before mid-2010.

And since they did not want to make their wedding a big event, they organized a secret wedding, inviting only close friends and family members. The outline also reveals that they wed in 2014.

But despite a few online magazines reporting that Booth is married to Wadlow, the actress, however, has never confirmed that she is married and that Wadlow is her spouse. 

Therefore, with no revelations about her husband and married life, her relationship status is as cloudy as ever.

Booth Wished Troy Dyer Was Her Boyfriend

On August 7, 2014, actress Booth shared through Twitter that she wished Troy Dyer was her boyfriend after she watched the movie Boyhood.

In case you didn't know, Troy Dyer is a fictional character in the movie Reality Bites played by Ethan Hawke.

Lindy Booth tweets about her fantasy boyfriend Troy Dyer. (Photo: Twitter)

Besides that, she has never revealed that she had a boyfriend or was dating someone.

Booth Career Rocketed after 'The Famous Jett Jackson'

The Canadian actress rose to fame with her portrayal of character Riley Grant on the Disney Channel series The Famous Jett Jackson, which aired from 1998 to 2001 for three seasons. Since then, her fame has only rocketed up.

In 2001, she played Lana Turner in the television film Life with Judy Garland: Me and My Shadows.

Likewise, in 2004, she acted as Nicole in the remake of Dawn of the Dead. After a few years in 2008, Booth starred in the thriller Behind the Wall.

She was back again on the sets of the television series The Librarians in 2014. The show gathered interest from viewers and boosted Booth's popularity.

She Attended the T.A. Blakelock High School

Booth was born on April 2, 1979, in Oakville, Ontario, Canada.

She stands tall at the height of  5 feet 7 inches (1.74 m), is white by ethnicity, and is of Canadian nationality.

Moreover, the talented actress was interested in drama from a very young age. Therefore, her high school teacher even bothered to find an agent for her after she graduated from high school.

She attended the T.A. Blakelock High School in Canada before graduating in 1998.
