Early Life

Jeffrey Lewis was born in 1970 in Orange County, California. He graduated from Mater Dei High School in 1988. He attended the University of Southern California and Chapman University, where he studied pre-law and political science.

In late 2008, Ashley Jensen and Terence Beesley, who lived next to a house in Los Feliz that Lewis was renovating, obtained a restraining order against him. The dispute arose when Lewis’s deck extended beyond the property line onto Jensen and Beesley’s property.

Beesley and Jensen sued and were awarded $100,000 for an easement. Lewis reacted negatively, prompting Beesley and Jensen to obtain a restraining order. 2009, the parties settled, agreeing on a $30,000 easement and allowing the deck to remain.

Career

Jeffrey Lewis began his career as a real estate agent after graduating from college in 1993. Before becoming a well-known house flipper and interior designer in the United States, he studied pre-law and political science at USC and Chapman University.

In 2009, he started his design firm. The following year, House Beautiful approached him to design their “Kitchen of the Year,” which was showcased at Rockefeller Center. Since 2013, he has also developed his home product brands, including rugs, paints, doors, and tiles.

His show, “I am flipping Out,” premiered in 2007 and focuses on Lewis’ house flipping projects and interior design business. The tenth season recently premiered in August 2017.

The show provides insights into his daily activities in real estate and the design firm while addressing his struggles with obsessive-compulsive behavior and his life outside the office.

In 2012, Lewis had a spinoff show called “Interior Therapy with Jeff Lewis,” aired on Bravo for two seasons. Additionally, he was a regular panelist on the show “Property Envy.”

Personal Life

Lewis also has a romantic involvement with Gage Edward, his business manager. Through a surrogate, the couple welcomed a child born last year.

Lewis was discovered to have an obsessive-compulsive personality disorder in his twenties, it is revealed.

His estimated net worth is a whopping $20 million. He also worked as a real estate agent in his early years, but after discovering his love, he made great strides.
