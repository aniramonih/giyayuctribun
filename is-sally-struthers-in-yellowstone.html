Is Sally Struthers in Yellowstone?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 19, 2024

American actress Sally Struthers is not a part of the Yellowstone cast.

The role of Caroline Warner, the fierce CEO of Market Equities in the series, is masterfully portrayed by the talented Jacki Weaver.

While Sally Struthers and Jacki Weaver share a few physical traits, such as being blondes with raspy voices, it’s crucial to distinguish between the two actresses. At 76 years old, they have both been praised for their enduring talent and captivating performances, earning them the nickname of “spiritual twins” due to their resemblance.

Jacki Weaver, hailing from Australia, has carved a formidable reputation in the world of entertainment. Known for her roles in the critically acclaimed “Silver Linings Playbook” and the Australian neo-noir crime drama “Animal Kingdom,” Weaver has proven her versatility and garnered Oscar nominations for Best Supporting Actress for both films.

Beyond the screen, Weaver boasts an extensive background in stage performances, adding depth and richness to her craft.

Prior to joining the cast of “Yellowstone,” she graced audiences with her presence in notable projects like “Blunt Talk,” “Gracepoint,” and “Perpetual Grace, LTD.”
