Alexis Bledel Plastic Surgery Before and After

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Kimberly Alexis Bledel was born on September 16, 1981 in Houston, Texas. After being scouted at a shopping mall, she worked as a fashion model and later attended Page Parkes Center for Modeling and Acting. She also studied at New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts for a year.

She then gained recognition for her role as Rory Gilmore in the TV series Gilmore Girls, which she played from 2000 to 2007. She also achieved mainstream fame on the big screen as Lena Kaligaris in 2005’s The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants and 2008’s The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants 2.

Has Alexis Bledel had Plastic Surgery?

Even the cute 32-year-old actress has been rumored of undergoing plastic surgery. According to some reports, the Gilmore Girls star has had a rhinoplasty procedure to enhance her nose’s shape. The alleged procedure created a very subtle result that no trace of a nose job could be easily noticed. Most people are hesitant to believe this claim as Bledel doesn’t seem to fit the type of someone who’s into plastic surgery.

Her beauty seems so raw and exquisite that to have it interfered with plastic surgery would certainly just taint her natural attractiveness. Fans also defended her, saying that up until now Bledel’s nose has distinctive imperfections that wouldn’t be there if she really underwent a cosmetic operation.
