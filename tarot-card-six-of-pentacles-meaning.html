Six of Pentacle’s Meanings in A Tarot Reading

The Six of Pentacles tarot card represents the arrival of charity or a time of generosity. This card appears in the Minor Arcana, and it shows that wealth will be shared evenly and can represent our financial status finally coming into balance. We may not be wealthy, but our incoming wealth will at least match our bills at last.

Six of Pentacles Tarot Card Meanings – Upright

Overview:

The arrival of the Six of Pentacles in a tarot reading indicates that generosity will play heavily into the current circumstances. These circumstances may involve you being the generous hand giving out wealth or being on the receiving end of wealth to help you through your hard times. The wealth involved can include a wealth of wisdom or knowledge.

Past Events:

You’ve been living on the generosity of another person, and it’s been helping you through this difficult time. However, you need to realize that this help is temporary, and while it has lent some ease to this time, it’s not going to last forever.

Present Events:

There is someone in your life who needs your generosity and assistance to get through a hard time. You’ve been on the fence about this, but know that it’s a good investment, they won’t take advantage of the situation, and it will pay off in the long run.

Future Events:

You’ve been struggling, doing everything you can, but you aren’t quite cutting. Do not fear; there is ease coming in your future.

Someone will be offering you help to see you through your difficulties. It’s not a long-term solution, but they’ll offer it without resentment.

Yes/No Interpretation: 

Yes, you should help them out, it’s a good direction to go in.  Know it’s a good investment of your time and emotions.

Six of Pentacles Tarot Card Meanings – Reversed

Overview:

The arrival of the Six of Pentacles is a warning, representing that you are dubious about the prospects of money lent out being returned. Know that if you are in financial trouble, this is a sign that now is not the time to be generous but to hold on to what you have.

This can also represent a problem with debt and a tendency to dig yourself into it. At its worst, it represents charity with strings attached, more than the simple expectation of repayment.

Past Events:

You’ve been having trouble hanging on to your money, so many hands and such a big heart have had you giving it out, or those credit cards have been just a little more tempting than you’ve been able to avoid.

Present Events:

Someone is petitioning to borrow money from you, and this is a warning against meeting them on this and loaning the money. If you do, do so with the knowledge that they won’t be paying it back anything like on time.

Future Events:

There is money that you’ve lent out that you’re expecting to see back. This is a warning to prepare for the eventuality that it won’t. Something is going to happen that will overcome the person’s best intentions of repaying you.

Yes/No Interpretation:

No, you should not extend the hand of generosity at this time, you will not see a return on it.

Summary

The six Pentacles signify the right kind of attitude that can help you find peace and love. Also, it teaches you the source of achieving the right goals while trying to help others achieve what they want. So, giving and receiving will be there to assist you in getting the right reward. Learn to assist others and let go of selfishness. Forge for what will make you realize your goals.
