Maria Pitillo began her career in suburban New Jersey, landing her first gig in a Pepto Bismol commercial. This led to other commercial roles, eventually securing a spot on the ABC soap opera Ryan’s Hope.

Her portrayal of Nancy Don (Lewis) on the show ran until 1989, establishing her as a recognized face on television.

In the 1990s, Pitillo moved to Hollywood, starring in several TV dramas and made-for-TV movies. Notable mentions include South of Sunset, Middle Ages, and Escape from Terror: The Teresa Stamper Story.

Her big break came with the FOX sitcom Partners, where she played Alicia Sondergard alongside Jon Cryer and Tate Donovan.
