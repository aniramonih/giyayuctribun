LIL KIM CHEERS ON DAUGHTER AT ROOKIE USA FASHION SHOW

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Lil Kim cheered on daughter, Royal Reign, as she  made her runway debut at the 13th Annual Rookie USA Fashion Show at Iron 23 on September 06, 2023 in New York City.

“I’m such a stage mom. My baby killed it. I’m sooo proud of her,” Lil Kim gushed in her Instaram stories, sharing several photos of her darling girl.

Royal joined an illustrious lineup of celebrity offspring gracing the catwalk for the 13th Annual Rookie USA Fashion Show, presented by Haddad Brands. The event showcased the latest fashion trends and must-have gear, featuring clothing, accessories, and footwear from renowned global brands like Nike, Jordan, Levi’s®, Converse, and Hurley.

Haileigh Vasquez, New York’s very own Kidfluencer, charmed the audience as the host, adding her unique charisma to the event. The stage came alive with captivating performances by Sophia Rita and the talented local Knicks City Kids, while DJ Sophia Rocks provided the perfect musical backdrop for the runway extravaganza.

Beyond the glamour and high fashion, the event had a noble philanthropic mission at its core. The proceeds from the show went towards supporting Moms for Moms NYC, on behalf of NY Knicks star Julius Randle. Moms for Moms aims to empower single mothers by providing essential postpartum and newborn items, offering a lifeline to those facing the challenges of poverty.

Lil Kim shares her daughter with rapper Mr Papers. The former couple celebrated their daughter’s 9th birthday this past June. To commemorate the occasion, Lil’ Kim took to Instagram to share a series of pictures that offered a glimpse into Royal’s 8th birthday celebration from the previous year.

The artist offered an explanation for sharing these throwback party pictures, revealing that she hadn’t had the time to arrange a celebration for this year.

“Last year my baby’s birthday was everything. Thanks to all of our family and friends and Jennifer @wearegrandscaleevents my awesome party planner girl who put this event together so my baby could have one of the most memorable, happiest, fun-filled birthdays ever,” she wrote.
