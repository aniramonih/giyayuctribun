With a career beyond Hollywood, Duane Martin has established himself as a true maverick. Aside from his acting career, he is well-respected as a writer and producer and has impeccable comedic timing, per his IMDb profile.

Duane Martin is a divorced dad of two

Duane Martin was married to former 'Martin' and 'My Wife and Kids' star Tisha Campbell. After over two decades of marriage, Campbell declared her divorce from Martin in 2018. Will Smith and Duane Martin's long-lasting friendship

The enduring friendship between Will Smith and Duane Martin lies at the core of the present controversy. The two formed a deep bond when they co-starred in the episode 'It Is Better To Have Loved and Lost It…' of 'Fresh Prince of Bel-Air'. While new allegations by Brother Bilaal have added fuel to the fire, the couple's staunch denial of any romantic involvement hasn't done anything to stop the speculations.

Years of unrelenting rumors

Initial reports regarding Will Smith and Duane Martin appeared in 1993, when they appeared together in the movie 'Six Degrees of Separation', where Will portrayed a gay con artist. Smith and Martin became close while working on 'Fresh Prince of Bel-Air' at around the same time. Their acquaintance gave rise to speculation of a potential covert romantic involvement, albeit it's unclear where these reports originated.

Rumors regarding Will Smith's sexuality

The question of Will Smith's sexual preference has long been the subject of gossip, with 'Will Smith gay' being a popular search term. Although Smith appeared to be happy in his marriage to Jada Pinkett Smith, rumors concerning Smith's personal life are still a hot topic in the media. The conjecture has been heightened by the recent mention of Duane Martin in relation to these rumors.

Allegations reappeared after Duane Martin and Tisha Campbell filed for divorce

The rumors were dormant for several years after Martin wed Tisha Campbell and Smith married Jada Pinkett Smith. The accusations surfaced once more in 2020, not long after Martin and Campbell filed for divorce. At the time, Campbell posted on Instagram, "Don’t have to say a word...
