Who Plays Spencer Paysinger's Wife, Blair Paysinger's Role In All American?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 26, 2024

American graphic designer, Blair Paysinger is the co-founder and chief executive officer of a comprehensive marketplace, Post 21. She handling the tradition of building community with modern design products that her granddad started years ago. 

Blair came into the limelight after getting hitched with former American football linebacker, Spencer Paysinger. More upon this, her beloved husband is currently starring in the Netflix sports drama series, All American, based on his true-life story since 2018.

How Blair First Met Her Husband, Spencer Paysinger?

32-years-old, Blair met Spencer Paysinger for the first time at Beverly Hills High School based in California in 2003. At that time, they both were freshmen, but he was one years older than her. 

During their first interaction, Blair did not remember the exact moment, when Spencer was trying to say something when she walked up. Later, she turned behind him and found out that he was asking for directions. She then provided him the location of the Athletic Department of BHHS at the Main Hallway.

The duo then became good friends at first and then, they found that they are made for each other. Furthermore, they expressed their feelings for each other at the high school's prom date.

After dating for many years, Blair's beau, Spencer made a surprising proposal for her. He first convinced her brother and then, her mom while she was planning a family game night. 

When they were playing taboo at the end of the night, Spencer got the card "wedding". And at that moment, he got down on his knees and proposed her for marriage. It was totally surprising for her and it was an awkward moment for her, but she happily accepted his proposal by saying, "yes!"

Lavish Wedding Ceremony

Find Your Hill Top co-owner, Blair and high school sweetheart, Spencer walked down the aisle on 28th May 2016. Moreover, their wedding was in a Bohemian-themed at La Quinta Resort & Club, A Waldorf Astoria Resort in California’s Palm Springs.

Blair wore a modern two-piece gown with a Chantilly lace crop top and silk organza skirt with an illusion veil at her nuptial. Meanwhile, her groom stunned in a blue grey classic tuxedo designed with a vintage pearl tie bar and suede loafers.

Become Parents of Two Children, Cairo & Madden

Black Love brand ambassador, Blair and former New York Giants linebacker, Spencer welcomed two children, Cairo Alaïa Paysinger (born on 15th May 2017) and Madden Creux Paysinger (born in July 2019). As of now, their older daughter is 4 years old while their younger son is only 2 years old.

At the moment, the couple are raising their two kids in a healthy environment. Currently, they resides in the greater Los Angeles bureau area of California, where The Foster actress, Sherri Saum also lives.

How Rich is Blair Paysinger?

Los Angeles, California-based graphic designer, Blair managed social media and advertising projects as an intern at Citizen Group in 2010. She later served as a sales representative for high street brand, Allsaints US from 2010 to 2011.

Blair, further, joined an eco-stylist brand, Alternative Apparel as a design intern from July 2013 to August 2014. She then established a black owned modern shop, Post 21 along with her mother in 2019.

Paysinger's current salary as a chief executive officer at Post 21 is $48,000 per annum. As of 2021, her net worth is around $380,000, which is less than Hallie Gnatovich's total fortune.

Earnings & Net Worth of Blair's Husband, Spencer

Blair's husband, Spencer earned $2,829,000 for 4 seasons with New York Giants from 2011 to 2014. He then signed a two year contract worth $1,585,000 with the Miami Dolphins for 2015 - 2016 season.

Paysinger then garnered $136,764 for playing as a New York Jets and Carolina Panthers linebacker in 2017. Throughout his NFL career, he made a total earnings of $4,550,764. Reportedly, his net worth is $1.5 Million in 2021.

Blair Paysinger's Short Bio

Spencer Paysinger's wife, Blair was born on 10th August 1989 in Los Angeles, California, USA. She received the maiden name "Bordenave-Duckworth" from her beloved father. However, there were some rumors that her biological dad is Beverly Eagles of Duckworth's trainer, Billy Duckworth.

Blair's mother is Juana Williams is currently 58 years old and current business partner of Blair in Post 21 Shop. When she was young, she aimed to become a news anchor, but turned out to be software designer when she grown up.

Blair and her mom, Juana founded the black owned modern online market, Post 21 based in Los Angeles, California for over two years. This platform showcases all Black-owned brands encompassing fashion, beauty, home décor, and many others. 

Blair grew up with one younger brother, Alex Duckworth, whom many people also calls her fraternity twin brother. Her sibling is a fashion and lifestyle photographer, videographer, and editor specializing in digital content marketing.

Blair attended Beverly Hills High School based in California. Following her high school graduation, she enrolled at the Academy of Art University of San Francisco in 2007. She later graduated with a Bachelor in Advertising degree with a Concentration on Art Direction in 2012.
