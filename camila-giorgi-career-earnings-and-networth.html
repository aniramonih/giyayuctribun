Camila Giorgi career earnings and net worth

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Camila Giorgi is best recognized as an Italian professional tennis player. She has a career-high singles ranking of world No. 26, achieved on 22 October 2018. She is the current Italian No. 2, after Martina Trevisan.

After winning her first ITF title in 2009, Giorgi made her Grand Slam main-draw debut at the 2011 Wimbledon Championships. Giorgi reached the fourth round of the 2012 Wimbledon Championships in just her second appearance at the tournament. After her successful run at the championships, she made her top-100 debut in the WTA rankings.

What is Camila Giorgi net worth?

Giorgi’s net worth is expected to be around $2 million. She also received approximately $5.5 million in prize money at the end of 2022. She turned professional in 2006 and has since won numerous matches in ITF Circuits and then in WTA.

How much does Camila Giorgi make a year?

Giorgi’s salary is under review, however, she has a racquet sponsorship contract with Yonex and uses the Yonex VCORE 100 racquet. However, she previously used Babolat racquets.
