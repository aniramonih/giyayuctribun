Early Life & Biography

James Van Der Beek was born in Cheshire, Connecticut, on March 8, 1977. He is the son of Melinda and James Van Der Beek. Melinda is a former dancer, and gymnastics teacher, and James is a cellular phone executive and former baseball pitcher. He is of Dutch ancestry.

When he was 15, he moved to New York City so that he can get an agent and try out in professional acting. He made is debut in Broadway in 1993 at the age of 16. When he was 17, he attended high school at Cheshire Academy. He participated in an all-male acapella group when he attended Drew University. He attended that university on an academic scholarship but later dropped out to pursue acting.
