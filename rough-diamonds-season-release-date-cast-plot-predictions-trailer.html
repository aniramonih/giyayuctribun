Rough Diamonds Season 2: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Storyline And Other Updates

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Crime thrillers have to be the most popular genres to binge on. Not only do they waken the detective skills in you, but they are also a perfect depiction of “It is not always what you think it is”. Routing for a person you presume innocent until they are prove guilty has to be one of the disappointing scenarios, but at least they help build character. One of the series that will have you on the edge of your seat is the Rough Diamonds. The show is a collaboration between Netflix and EEn and stars Kevin Janssens, Ini Massez and Robbie Cleiren. It explores the unorthodox Jewish family in the diamond trade. Business is booming until bad decisions are made, and sacrifices must be made to salvage the business. Here is everything we know about the upcoming show.

Rough Diamonds Season 2 Release Date: When will it air?

The gritty drama has been scheduled for an April 21st release date and will be exclusively available on Netflix. The series is expected to have eight episodes, and currently, we are unsure whether the episodes will drop on the same day or weekly. In the meantime, you should get a Netflix subscription as we are unsure whether the show will be available for all the free plan users. A Netflix subscription is available for as low as $14.99  monthly or a premium at $19.99 a month.

Rough Diamonds Season 2 Plot: What will Happen?

The series plot revolves around Antwerp’s highly unorthodox Jewish family, famously known for trading diamonds. Antwerp is a rich ground full of a billion worthy sacred jewels, and the mighty toss their luck in it. One of the brothers runs the family business, but he makes bad and uncalculated financial decisions that lead the family into almost bankruptcy. His estranged brother must return home to salvage the family business before they face bankruptcy. From the released trailer, the series will feature death, grief morning and celebration. It will contain disagreements which we believe are between the two brothers or his business partners.

Rough Diamonds Season 2 Cast: Who will be in?

Casper Knopf stars as Tommy McCabe, Kevin Janssens as Noah Wolfson, Marie Vinck as Gila Wolfson, Ini Massez as Adina Glazer, Robbie Cleiren as Eli Wolfson, Yona Elian as Sarah Wolfson, Els Dottermans as Jo Smets and Jane Desmet as Rivki Wolfson. Other actors include Tine Joustra as Kerra, Gene Bervoets as Mathias Dumont, Kasper as Maarten Prinsen, Jeroen as Benny Feldman, Soroush Helali as Bujar, Alicia Andreis  Miri Wolfson, Jo Jochems as Schmulik Glazer.

Rough Diamonds Season 2 Trailer: When can we watch it?

Fortunately, the teaser for the Rough Diamonds Season 2 has already been released, and you will not have to wait forever for a sneak peek of the upcoming show. The series trailer starts with what seems like a funeral, with everybody wearing all black. It then progresses to business, feuds, disagreements, diamonds, money and family dynamics. For more details on the show’s second season, you may want to check out the already-released trailer. Before the series is released, you can check out the show’s first season if you have not already. The series is available on Netflix, and you will need a  subscription to watch the show alongside the other thriller series that Netflix offers.
