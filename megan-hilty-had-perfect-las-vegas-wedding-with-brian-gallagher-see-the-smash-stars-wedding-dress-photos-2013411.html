Megan Hilty Wedding Dress Photo: Smash Star Talks Vegas Nuptials

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Megan Hilty‘s wedding dress was a smashing success! The 32-year-old former Smash actress married boyfriend Brian Gallagher in Las Vegas on Saturday, Nov. 2. Us Weekly has obtained photos of the gorgeous bride and her handsome groom from their intimate ceremony at The Venetian Chapel.

Hilty wore a stunning white Badgley Mischka gown with a silver embellished bodice and lace train. The bride carried a white floral bouquet and styled her short blonde hair in soft curls. Gallagher, a 33-year-old fellow actor, posed alongside his bride in a black tux.

“We shared a perfect weekend with our family,” Hilty, who currently stars on NBC’s Sean Saves the World, told Us exclusively of her wedding. “To us it’s more about the marriage than the actual wedding, which is why we decided to skip the whole engagement thing. We couldn’t be happier!”

“It was only our immediate family — we called them about a week ago and told them,” she explained. “We invited everyone but didn’t expect anyone to show up since it was so last minute . . . but they did! It was perfect!”
