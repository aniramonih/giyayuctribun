Who is Riva Arora? Wiki, Bio, Family &amp; Facts About Actress

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Riva Arora (born February 1, 2010) is a famous Indian child actress, model, social media influencer, Instagram personality, TikTok star, Youtuber, and entrepreneur from New Delhi, India. This talented young actress earned a huge fan following in a very short period. Let me tell you that, she started working in the entertainment industry when she was only 1 year old. 

Apart from movies, she has also worked in various commercial advertisements ad modeling agencies. At a very young age, Riva Arora worked along with many famous Bollywood celebrities including Sridevi, Bonney Kapoor, Ranbir Singh, and others. 

Not only acting, but she has also a large fan following on her different social media handles. As of April 2021, there are over 3 million followers on her official Instagram account. She also runs her own self-titled Youtube channel. Scroll down to know more information regarding this young multi-talented girl. 

Wiki / Quick Facts 

Biography 

Famous Indian child actress, Riva Arora took birth to her parents on Monday, February 1st, 2010, in New Delhi, India. She was born into a well-settled Hindu family. According to her date of birth, Riva Arora’s age is 11 years old (as of 2021). She cuts her birthday cake on 1st February every year along with her family and friends. 

Riva has a great interest in the entertainment industry since childhood. Her mother always supports her in this field. Even, her mom also sent her pictures to various modeling agencies. According to the sources, Riva completed her primary education at a local private school in Delhi. 

After that, she moved to Mumbai along with her family to make a bright future in the entertainment industry. At present, she is pursuing her further studies at a reputed school in Mumbai. Along with her studies, she also focuses on her acting career. 

Family & Caste 

According to the sources, Riva Arora belongs to s Khatri caste and follows the Hinduism religion. 

Parents & Siblings

As per the reports, Riva Arora is the first daughter of her parents. Talking about her family then, her father (name not known) is a successful businessman.

According to her social media profile, she considers “Gufran Ansari” (TikTok star) as her elder brother. She also made lots of videos with him. 

Career (As an actress)

It would not be wrong to say that Riva was born to work in the entertainment industry, because she made her Bollywood debut when she was only one and a half years old. Yes! Rewa Arora’s first film is Rockstar (2011). 

After that, she has featured in so many movies like Naina, Mard Ko Dard Nahi Hota, Bharat, Uri: The Surgical Strike. According to the media reports, Riva has also worked in her first Hollywood film Best Friends in 2018. 

In the same year, she also made her television debut with the TV show “Papa Mere Hero Hiralal”. Apart from TV and movies, Riva Arora has also featured in so many commercial advertisements and promotions.
