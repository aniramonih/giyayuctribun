Let sinners sin and let believers believe, you can’t be somewhere in between, pretending to be holier than though and white as snow, yet deep down you are a savage, thirsty and a greedy son of man, hiding his claws so as to manipulate people. Being a man of God comes with respect, dignity and lots of expectations from the society, but these men have broken that trust.

KTN journalists (KTN’s Inside Story) Mohamed Ali and John Allan inquired intensively into and exposed the malpractice of Fake pastors in Kenya, and other controversial issues concerning clergymen who are taking advantage of their flock to enrich themselves in Kenya. The expose, nicknamed “Makri ya Injili” in Swahili and “Prayer Predators” in English, showed clearly the complete deceptive methods that Salvation Healing Ministries pastors use to lure and exploit their over-trusting flock. The two journalists revealed expert means used by pastors of exploiting their flock in the name of God.
