PHOTOS VIDEO Shira Scott Astrof from The Bachelor 16 with Ben Flajnik

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 26, 2024

One of the 25 women competing for the heart of The Bachelor Ben Flajnik in season 16 of the popular ABC reality series won’t have any difficulty getting used to being in front of the camera, and that’s because 31-year-old Shira Scott Astrof is a professional actress with quite an extensive resume – including a rather infamous role on NBC’s The Office!

Shira, who now goes by just Shira Scott, played Andy Bernard’s girlfriend Jamie in The Office Season 3 episode 20 “Product Recall.” That may not sound all that impressive or memorable, but in that episode Andy is shocked when he runs into Jamie while making a sales call at a local high school. As it turns out, Jamie is a student! It was something the ol’ ‘Nard Dog probably should have realized when Jamie told him she was a part time yogurt chef, or maybe when he went out with her friend to smash mailboxes.
