Melissa Russo Bio | Wiki

Melissa Russo is an American Journalist and Government Affairs reporter. Currently, she works at WNBC-TV News Channel 4 in New York City. Melissa joined WNBC-TV in September 1998. Previously, she served as the co-anchor for the News 4 New York at the 6 pm and 11 pm Saturday newscasts.

Because of her reputation for fairness and substantive reporting, Melissa has received unprecedented access to places where no other TV reporters have been permitted. Melissa is the recipient of two Edward R. Murrow awards.

She won in 2014 for “Closing Time: Due Process Denied.” This is an investigation that exposed budget cuts were resulting in children being separated unnecessarily from their parents. Melissa’s report resulted in New York’s chief judge extending family court hours to avoid further trauma to children.

Melissa Russo Education
