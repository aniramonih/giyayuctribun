Who is Milan Lucic Wife? The Woman Behind the NHL Star

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Milan Lucic, the renowned professional ice hockey player, is not just known for his impressive career in the NHL but also for his personal life, particularly his marriage.

In this article, we delve into the life of Milan Lucic’s wife, exploring their journey together and the role she plays in his life both on and off the ice.

Who is Milan Lucic Wife?

Milan Lucic’s wife is Brittany Carnegie. The couple has been together for 11 years, and their relationship has been a notable part of Lucic’s life outside his professional hockey career.

Brittany Carnegie, while primarily known for her marriage to the NHL star, maintains her own identity and interests, contributing to their family life and community. She has been a supportive figure in Lucic’s life, both during his professional highs and lows, and they share a strong and committed partnership.

Milan Lucic and Brittany Carnegie Relationship

Milan Lucic and Brittany Carnegie met when Lucic was establishing himself in his hockey career. The couple got married on July 19, 2012. The couple has been happily married for more than ten years. Milan Lucic shared a post on his Instagram to celebrate his tenth anniversary.
