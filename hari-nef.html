Hari Nef is an American actress, model, and writer who is best known for portraying roles such as Gittel in Transparent, L. Roy Dunham in The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, and Blythe in You. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows such as Camping, Acting for a Cause, Room 104, And Just Like That…, Mapplethorpe, Barbie, Assassination Nation, and many more. Hari made her runway debut in 2015 at the New York Fashion Week and later became the first transgender woman signed to IMG Models. She has endorsed brands like L’Oréal Paris and has been featured in magazines such as Frische Magazine and Elle.
