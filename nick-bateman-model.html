Nick Bateman is a Canadian model, actor, and social media personality who first rose to fame through Instagram. He was soon diverted towards the action and has appeared in films like Hobo with a Shotgun and Winter Wedding. Being a Karate trainee, Nick posts a lot of karate tricks videos on his Instagram and the videos go viral instantly. His Instagram has amassed more than 7 million followers.

    Personal Trainer

    As per an interview in May 2017, Nick’s workout routine was wrapped around his work schedule. He tried to work out 3 to 4 times a week. His regime mostly included resistance and weight training in the gym.

    He also said that he ate fairly healthy and drank a lot of water to keep his body hydrated. He tried to eat a lot of vegetables and maintained a high protein diet. Nick claimed that he was very good at self-control when it comes to having cheat meals and he only had one ‘cheat meal’ a week usually over the weekend.

    Nick Bateman Facts

  • As a child, Nick was a huge Ninja Turtle fan and to become one he joined the Karate classes at the age of 4.
  • His martial arts training was in Gōjū-ryū karate and bō staff.
  • In one of his interviews, he claimed that he had Attention-deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder and Karate helped him concentrate and focus.
  • He went on to win 4 world titles in 15-17 age division of the National Blackbelt League.
  • He is also the winner of 2 Sports Martial Arts Viewers Choice Awards.
  • In 2004, he participated in the ESPN International Best of the Best Martial Arts competition where he stood 2nd against 10 world champions at the age of 17.
  • While he was in Miami attending the ESPN International Best of the Best Martial Arts competition, the tournament organizer jokingly entered him in a modeling contest where he stood 5th among hundred models.
  • After he left competing in Karate, he went towards modeling and acting career.
  • One of the judges of the modeling competition introduced him to Calvin Klein but Nick did not recognize him thinking that he was just an old man named Calvin.
  • In Toronto, he was signed with Elmer Olsen Model Management and traveled to Italy, Germany, and New York for modeling purposes.
  • He worked with top photographer Bruce Weber in 2009 for the Abercrombie & Fitch campaign.
  • He opened his own karate school in Burlington in 2008 which was his dream but had to close it when he was signed for the movie Hobo With a Shotgun.
  • In 2018, he launched his own clothing line with the name DTLA.
  • He has two pets Yorkies dogs, named Joey and Keeva and both of them have their Instagram accounts.
