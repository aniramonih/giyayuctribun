Ami Brown of 'Alaskan Bush People' Is a Proud Mother of 7 Children Meet All of Them

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

Television actress Ami Brown and her husband, Billy Brown, are parents to seven children, including Rain Brown and Joshua Brown.

Ami Brown, known for the television reality series, "Alaskan Bush," is married to Billy Brown, with the couple tying the knot on June 16, 1979. Their union is blessed with seven children.

The Brown kids are sons Matt, Joshua "Bam Bam," Bear Brown, Gabe, Noah, and daughters Snowbird "Birdy," and Rain.

Ami Brown at the Discovery Communications portion of the 2014 Winter Television Critics Association tour on January 9, 2014. | Photo: Getty Images

RAIN BROWN

Rain Brown is the last born child in the Brown Family and is the family's princess. She is a kind and loving person whose favorite color is pink. Reading is one of her favorite hobbies, with the Nancy Drew Mysteries among her best reads.

Rain was born on November 23, 2002, and describes herself on her Instagram bio as a child of God, inspirational writer, and fulltime warrior. She is also a mother of two dogs, Jakson and Bardum.

JOSHUA "BAM BAM" BROWN

Bam Bam is the second-born son of the Browns. He grew up being an energetic and active kid. He loves to hunt, fish, read books, and is also a free thinker. Bam Bam departed his family's show in 2017 when he found love with a city girl.

He said the woman, Allison Kagan, stirred something inside him and awakened his soul. Bam Bam hoped that they could blend their lives and that he could take her back to Alaska with him.

MATT BROWN

Matt went to rehab twice, first in 2016, and again in 2018. His family supported him through it all, saying that they wanted him to do what needed to be done to get better.

BEAR BROWN

Bear Brown is a happy being who loves to hunt, fish, or perform other activities. He also has a great likeness to the ocean. On his Instagram page, Bear states that he enjoys climbing trees and hanging upside down, and mostly loves being extreme. Bear also said that he was looking for the girl who would howl back.

GABE BROWN

Gabe is the fourth born son of Ami and Billy and was only 3-month-old when his family moved onboard their first fishing boat. This made him have a strong bond with the ocean. He loves to smile and crack jokes, is hardworking, and strong. Gabe also loves to learn new things and is fluent in sign language.

NOAH BROWN

Noah Brown is the kind of person that loves to take things apart and put them together just to see how they work. He loves to dream and invent new things and hopes to travel the world and find his dream woman.

SNOWBIRD "BIRDIE" BROWN

Birdie is the first daughter of the Brown Clan. Despite being born after five boys, she grew up to become an independent lady and loves to spend her days outdoors. Birdie loves to draw and loves animals.
