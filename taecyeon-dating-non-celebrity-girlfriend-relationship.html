OK Taecyeon Is Dating Non-Celebrity Girlfriend

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 10, 2024

It's time for some revelation. The main rapper of Kpop group 2 PM, OK Taecyeon's love life, has come forth in media. Taecyeon's agency confirmed the news on 23 June 2020, stating that he is seeing someone who is non-celebrity.

Several rumors had surfaced that cited Taecyeon was seen in Pyeongchang, Gangwon Province, in South Korea with his non-celebrity girlfriend. Later, his agency, named 51K, responded to the stories and confirmed the affairs of OK Taecyeon. It released a statement saying:

"It’s true that Taecyeon is currently in a relationship with a non-celebrity."

However, the company denied pouring additional information about it. It mentioned, 'We are cautious about saying anything as this is his personal privacy and his girlfriend is not a celebrity.'

OK Taecyeon and his non-celebrity girlfriend (©: Twitter/Ok2PMForever)

The famous 2 PM member Taecyeon later talked about his girlfriend in an exclusive interview with Sports Chosun. As per the reports, the love interest of the rapper is three years younger than him, which means she is 29-year-old. She works as an ordinary office worker.

Taecyeon and his girlfriend started dating in 2017, which marks their three years of the romantic relationship, as of 2020. The reports further claim that Taecyeon and his 29 years old ladylove went to the deepest bond even when Taecyeon served in the military. Taecyeon made his way to the military as an active-duty soldier on 4 September 2017.

After finishing his training session on 18 October 2017, he was placed at the 9th Infantry Division in Gyeonggi Province. He served as a military instructor assistant.

Who Is Taecyeon?

Taecyeon is one of the KPop stars. He is a rapper of the South-Korean music group named 2 PM, famous for hit songs like My House, I'm Your Man, Take Off, Without You, Guilty Love, Beautiful, and many others.

Taecyeon is also an actor known for his works in movies and tv series Cinderella's Sister (2010), Dream High (2011), Marriage Blue (2013), Assembly (2015), House of the Disappeared (2017), The Game: Towards Zero (2020), and others.

Taecyeon was born on 27 December 1988 in Busan, South Korea, and grew up with his sister OK Jihyen. He attended Dankook University, where he studied the Business program. As of 2020, he is presented by an agency called 51K.
