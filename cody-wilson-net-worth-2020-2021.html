Early Life & Biography

Cody Rutledge Wilson was born in Little Rock, Arkansas, on January 31, 1988. He completed his schooling from the Cabot High School in Cabot, Arkansas, where he was the student body president.

He later attended the University of Central Arkansas (UCA), where he had a scholarship and a bachelor’s degree in English in 2010. Wilson was the president of UCA’s Student Government Association and a member of Sigma Phi Epsilon fraternity. At that time, he traveled to many countries, including China, with UCA’s study-abroad program.

Wilson also attended the University of Texas School of Law in 2012 but dropped out the following year.

Career

Cody Wilson began the Wiki Weapon Project to raise funds for designing and releasing the files for a 3D printable gun along with Defense Distributed in 2012. Later, they were threatened by the American manufacturer of 3D printers Stratasys of legal action if Wilson didn’t return their leased 3D printer. The deal was canceled, and they confiscated the printer.

In 2013, Wilson test-fired a pistol called “the Liberator,” which was developed by Stratasys Dimension series. He then released the blueprints of the gun’s design online through a Defense Distributed website. Later, the State Department Office of Defense Trade Controls Compliance asked him to delete the files from the internet because it was a violation of the International Traffic in Arms Regulations. The following year, Defense Distributed started selling a miniature CNC mill named Ghost Gunner.

In the same year, along with Amir Taaki, Wilson founded Dark Wallet, which was a Bitcoin cryptocurrency wallet to help anonymize financial transactions. In 2017, he created the website Hatreon, to provide crowdfunding and payment services for someone who is blocked from platforms such as Kickstarter, Patreon, PayPal, and Stripe.
