Fatal Crash on LA 23 in Plaquemines Parish

Tragedy struck in the early hours of November 19, 2023, when a devastating accident occurred on LA 23 near Captain Larry Lane in Plaquemines Parish. This incident serves as a stark reminder of the importance of responsible driving and the devastating consequences that can result from impaired driving.

Details of the Incident

The incident involved a fatal two-vehicle crash that unfolded on LA 23. According to the initial investigation conducted by the Louisiana State Police Troop B, the collision occurred when a 2015 Jeep Cherokee driven by Peter Thomason from Devine, TX, collided with a 2016 Toyota Scion operated by Michael Carr. The investigation revealed that Thomason initiated a lane change, moving into the left lane before returning to the right lane. Unfortunately, this sudden maneuver resulted in a collision with the rear of Carr’s Toyota, causing it to veer off the road and collide with the concrete base of a utility pole.

Victim and Vehicles Involved

The tragic crash claimed the life of 32-year-old Michael Carr from Baytown, TX. Carr was driving the Toyota Scion at the time of the accident. Despite being properly restrained, he sustained fatal injuries and was pronounced dead at the scene. A passenger in the Toyota suffered minor injuries and was taken to a nearby local hospital for treatment.
