Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 5, 2024

Alan Jackson has three kids with his wife Denise. Mattie Denise Jackson, Dani Grace Jackson, and Alexandra Jane Jackson are their kids’ names.

On June 19, 1990, Mattie was born and is now 32 years old. On October 7, 2017, she married Ben Selecman, the love of her life, five years after receiving her degree from the University of Tennessee.

Unfortunately, though, her husband passed just a month before their first wedding anniversary. While on vacation in Florida, he sustained a catastrophic brain injury.

Alexandra was born on August 23, 1993, and she is now 28 years old. When a policeman stopped her and a buddy on the highway in 2013, she ended up in legal jeopardy.

She had been drinking illegally at the time and was seated in the passenger seat of the car. She was accused of resisting arrest, assault, and drinking alcohol while underage during a traffic encounter, according to Metro Nashville Police.

Dani is 24 years old and was born on August 28, 1997. The gap between her and Alexandra’s birthdays is just five days.
