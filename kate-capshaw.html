Kate Capshaw Age, Biography

Kate Capshaw entered the world for the first time on November 3, 1953, in Fort Worth, Texas, United States. Kate will be 70 years old in 2023, and her star sign is Scorpio, according to her birthdate. Her mother, Beverley Sue, worked as a travel agent, while her father, Edwin Leon Nail, worked for an airline.
