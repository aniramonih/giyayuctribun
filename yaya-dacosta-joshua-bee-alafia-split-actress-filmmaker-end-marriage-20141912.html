Yaya DaCosta, Joshua Bee Alafia Split: Actress, Filmmaker End Marriage

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Actress Yaya DaCosta is back on the market. The America’s Next Top Model alum and Big Words star, 32, has parted ways with her husband Joshua Bee Alafia, her rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

DaCosta, who next stars as Whitney Houston in the upcoming Lifetime biopic Whitney, married filmmaker Alafia in June 2012. No reason has been given for the split after two years of marriage.

A runner-up on Cycle 3 of ANTM, DaCosta gave birth to the couple’s son Sankara in September 2013. After competing on the Tyra Banks-led show, she went on to appear in episodes of All My Children and Ugly Betty, and star in 2013’s Lee Daniels’ The Butler.

Alafia and DaCosta, who exchanged vows in an outdoor wedding in Pennsylvania, have yet to comment on their breakup.
