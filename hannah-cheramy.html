Hannah Cheramy is a Canadian actress famous for her appearance in “The Elder” and “Van Helsing”. She began her career at the age of 7 in theatre and has grown to appear in a lot of movies and TV series, most recently, in an Epix horror series, “From”.

Hannah Cheramy was Born in Vancouver

Canadian actress, Hannah Cheramy was born on July 13, 2003, in Vancouver, Canada, to Louis Cheramy and his wife, Terry Cheramy. She is an only child.

The young Hannah started doing song and dance routines at the age of 3 and took interest in acting at the tender age of 7. She joined the local children’s theatre and became actively involved in the arts.

She attended the Burnaby North Secondary High School. While in High school, she wrote poems, sang in the music club, and was a member of the dance club. She was an all-around creative.

Hannah graduated from the University with a Bachelor of Film arts and is currently into acting full-time.

Hannah has Very Supportive Parents

After the young Hannah told her parents she wanted to make a career out of acting, her mother sent her photo to her friend who had a talent agency. It was from there that her journey to fame began.

Hannah got her first-ever role with the help of the talent agency. She started by getting roles in different commercials, before delving into TV and film proper.

She Began Her Acting Career in 2015

After High school, Hannah decided to pursue a career in acting. She got a role to play a role in the TV series “October Kiss”. Hannah played the character of Zoe Larson. Her outstanding performance in the TV series earned her a nomination for the Leo Awards, Young Artist Award, and Joey Award

Subsequently, she feature in several other movies including “My Sweet Audrina” 2016, she was 11 years old at that time. In that same year, she featured in “Summer Love” where she played the character Addison Sulliway.

However, she did not come into the limelight until her feature in the first season of the fantasy horror series, “Van Helsing” in 2016. She got the role after she made her entry in September 2016. She first appeared in 2 episodes. Subsequently, she has gone ahead to feature in more seasons of the show up until the fifth, appearing in over 10 episodes of the TV drama.

Her performance as Dylan in “Van Helsing” earned her a nomination for the Joey Awards. She appeared in Epix Horror series, “From” where she plays the role of Julie Matthews.

The 20-year-old Leads a Private Life

Hannah unlike many of her industry colleagues leads a very private life, hence little or nothing is known about her family and personal life.

She owns an Instagram where she has more than 9 thousand followers.  Hannah also owns a Twitter account with over a thousand followers. She uses her social media platforms to promote movies she features in but leaves out details about her life and family private and away from the prying eyes of the public.
