    Shin Dong-yup Facts

  • Along with Lee Jung, Kang Min-kyung, Kim Soo-mi, Lee Soo-hyuk, Kim Woo-bin, and Hong Jong-hyun, Shin Dong-yup co-starred as Dong-yup (the manager of a talent agency who is in charge of Vampire Voice and everything he says is a lie) in the 2011 comedy-drama television show Vampire Idol.
  • In 2015, he was seen as Hyung-geun (assistant director of 2 Days & 1 Night) in the comedy-drama television series The Producers. It stars Cha Tae-hyun, Gong Hyo-jin, Kim Soo-hyun, and Lee Ji-eun.
  • Shin Dong-yup appeared as himself in an episode of the television sitcom The Sound of Your Heart in 2016. Based on the webtoon of the same name, the show’s lead cast consists of Lee Kwang-soo and Jung So-min.
  • In 2018, he was cast as a guest as Immar’s voice in the romantic comedy-drama television series Wok of Love. The show stars Lee Jun-ho, Jang Hyuk, and Jung Ryeo-won.
  • Shin Dong-yup has been a recipient of various accolades such as the Baeksang Arts Award, KBS Entertainment Award, Cable TV Broadcasting Award, Korea Broadcasting Award, SBS Entertainment Award, SBS Drama Award, Style Icon Award, Korean Popular Culture and Arts Award, Mnet 20’s Choice Award, tvN10 Award, and Korea PD Award.
  • He was included in Forbes‘ “Korea Power Celebrity” list in 2014, 2016, 2017, and 2018.
  • In July 2023, it was confirmed that Shin Dong-yup had decided to part ways with his longtime agency, SM C&C, after 11 years. He first signed with the agency in 2012.
