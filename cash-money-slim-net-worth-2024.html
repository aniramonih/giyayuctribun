The Rise of Cash Money Records

The story of Cash Money Slim’s wealth begins with the rise of Cash Money Records, the label he co-founded with his brother, Bryan “Birdman” Williams. Established in 1991, the label quickly became a powerhouse in the hip-hop scene, especially in the South.

Early Successes and Expansion

Cash Money Records’ early success was driven by the talent of local artists and the Williams brothers’ business savvy. They secured distribution deals that favored the label, ensuring a larger cut of profits and control over their music.

Signing Big Names

The label’s reputation soared with the signing of major artists like Lil Wayne, Juvenile, and the Hot Boys. These artists became synonymous with the Cash Money brand and propelled the label to national fame.
