How Tall is Lil Wayne?

Lil Wayne is 5 feet 5 inches tall which is the same thing as a height of 1.65 m or 165 cm. The rapper visibly looks short and available statistics confirm it. The average American man is 5 feet 9 inches or 175 cm tall, this means that Lil Wayne is four inches shorter than the average American male.

The same situation also obtains in Hollywood. The height of the average Hollywood male star is put at 5 feet 10 inches or 178 cm. this means that Lil Wayne is five inches shorter than the average Hollywood male star.

How Tall Is Lil Wayne Compared To Drake?

Lil Wayne and Drake have worked on not less than 20 songs together. This has required them to feature together in numerous videos as well. Any keen watcher of these music videos will notice that Drake is visibly taller than Wayne. Drake is 6 feet or 1.83 cm tall. This means that Drake is seven inches taller than Lil Wayne.

Lil Wayne’s Height vs Wiz Khalifa’s Height

Wiz Khalifa is also a popular rapper. He has dropped several hit songs including Sucker for Pain, a collaboration with Lil Wayne. When it comes to their height, Wiz Khalifa is taller than Lil Wayne. The black and yellow crooner is 6 feet 4 inches or 1.93 m tall. It, therefore, means that Wiz Khalifa is 11 inches or almost a foot taller than Lil Wayne.

Is Lil Wayne’s Height More Than Future’s?

Even though their styles are not necessarily similar, Lil Wayne has worked together on multiple songs with rapper, Future. When it comes to their height, Future is 6 feet 2 or 1.88 m. This means that Lil Wayne is nine inches shorter than future.

Megan Thee Stallion vs Lil Wayne’s Height

Megan Thee Stallion is one of the most well-known female rappers in the world. She is a big fan of Lil Wayne and has previously described him as a big influence as well as one of her favorite rappers. Megan Thee Stallion is 5 feet 10 inches. This means that she is 5 inches taller than Lil Wayne.

Lil Wayne’s Height Compared to Jay-Z’s Height

Jay Z is 6 feet 2 inches or 1.88 m. This makes him nine inches taller than Lil Wayne. Both rappers have worked together on songs such as Mr. Carter and Hello Brooklyn. Lil Wayne has also talked about Jay-Z’s impact on his life.

Lil Wayne Weight and Other Body Measurements

Lil Wayne’s weight is estimated at 61 kg or 134 lbs. His other measurements are not available but a look at the rapper will show that he has maintained a lean frame over the years.
