Who is MyKayla Skinner?

MyKayla Skinner is an American artistic gymnast. She recently grabbed the attention by making the 2020 Olympic Games in Tokyo.

MyKayla Skinner- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Her birthdate is 09 Dec 1996 and she was born in Gilbert, Arizona, the United States as MyKayla Brooke Skinner Harmer. The name of her mother is Kym and her father is Chris.

MyKayla’s ancestry is Caucasian and has an older brother named Jeremy Skinner. She also has two former gymnast sisters Chelsea Skinner plus Katie Skinner.

As per her academics Skinner first attended Higley High School and then the University of Utah.

MyKayla Skinner- Professional Life, Career

MyKayla Skinner is a role model for all the gymnasts for competing and winning several competitions. During her career, she participated in World Championships and grabbed three gold medals for the team in 2014, 2015, & 2019.

She also won a bronze in the year 2014. Moreover, this player was also seen giving tough competition at  2014’s Pan American Championships. MyKayla also appeared at the FIG World Cup and won the NCAA Championships two times in 2017 and 2018.

As of now, MyKayla is competing at the 2020 Olympic Games representing her country the US. She came fifth in the all-around and was named as the plus-one specialist due to her amazing skills.

She also announced her retirement after the game and planned to complete her degree at the University of Utah.
