Snoop Dogg (born 20 October 1971) is a famous American rapper, singer, actor, songwriter, record producer, and entrepreneur from Long Beach, California city in the USA. He is well known in the public for his raps. He stepped in the music industry with the help of famous American rapper and singer “Dr. Dre”.

At that time, he used to write his own raps. In the year 1992, he has appeared in Dre’s song named “Deep Cover”. After that, in the year 1993, he launched his first album named “Doggystyle”. People loved this album so much. Snoop had also awarded so many popular awards for this album.

Then he released so many hits albums and raps. Apart from this, he has also appeared in the WWE Hall of Fame. Snoop Dogg has also featured in many shows of Wrestle Mania XXIV in 2016. The WWE WrestlerSasha Banks is his cousin sister. In 2020, Snoop cast his voice in the animated movie “The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run”.

The famous singer started his music career in the year 1992. The popular singer Dr, Dre helps him a lot to rose fame in the music industry. He has a writing talent in his hand from the beginning. People liked his album very much. After that, he released his second album “Tha Doggfather” in 1993. From this album, he gained huge fame and touched the height of success.

After that, he released so many hit albums like Reincarnated, Tha Blue Carpet Treatment, No Limit Top Dogg, etc. Apart from this, he also earned a lot of fame in the field of acting. In 1994, Snoop Dogg got an opportunity to worked in his first Hollywood movie named “Murder Was the Case”.

People liked his character in this movie very much. Then, he made his appearance in so many movies like The Wrecking Crew, Arthur, and the Invisibles, The Distortion of Sound, etc.

He earned the Net worth of $150 million (as in 2020) from his acting and music career. Because of a famous personality, he has also appeared in WWE Wrestle Mania. Snoop also invited to the Hall of Fame of WWE in 2016.

His 2019’s album “I Wanna Thank Me” got huge popularity. In 2020, his voice has selected for the animated movie named “The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run”.
