Kiara Advani Weight Loss Journey: Before And After Photos

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Kiara Advani Weight Loss Journey – Kiara Advani, an Indian actress, appears in Hindi and Telugu movies. She made her acting debut in the movie Fugly (2014). In the 2016 movie MS Dhoni: The Untold Story, she also portrayed MS Dhoni’s wife.

After appearing in the Netflix Anthology film Lust Stories, the actress became well-known (2018). In the movie, she played a wife who wasn’t satisfied romantically. She rose to fame after making an appearance in the love drama Kabir Singh (2019).

The actress from Kabir Singh has been nominated for and won numerous awards throughout her career, including best actress at the 2019 Asiavision Awards, 2020 International Indian Film Academy Awards, and 2020 Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards India.

The actress has now been a renowned name in the Bollywood film industry and maintains her body well to be in perfect shape and size. She has a strict routine to help her maintain her shape.

Kiara Advani Weight Loss Journey -Before And After Photos

Kira Advani has worked out well to pull the shape she is in today. Let’s find out about her weight loss journey and her before and after photos.

The actress is fitness-conscious and takes her shape and figure very seriously. She has adopted many strategies like regular exercise, yoga and workout, and change in diet routine to attain the body shape and size she is today.

Further, according to the actress, she is one of the few people who doesn’t get extensively affected by food consumption, enabling her to consume any food she wishes without extra stress. This adds benefit to her fitness maintenance.

To maintain her fitness, the actress has made several changes to her lifestyle, like having a flexible workout routine, pre-workout meals, and more. The actress has worked hard and has a fitness-dedicated journey. Below are some pictures showing her before and after photos.

Kiara Advani Diet And Workout Routine

Kiara Advani prefers a balanced diet with low sodium and has a maintained workout routine to take care of her fitness.

The actress’s favorite morning routine is lemon water, followed by fruits and oats. She prefers having both all-season and seasonal fruits in her breakfast. The actress prefers homemade food for her lunch, maintaining different vegetables daily.

The actress goes for omega-3 and protein for dinner and prefers seafood like salmon. Further, she loves having a pre-workout meal and generally includes slices of apple and peanut butter before going to the gym.

As a part of her workout, she follows routine gym-related activities. Besides, the actress also has a keen interest in physical activities like kickboxing. She often includes kickboxing in her fitness regime. Her gym activities vary, taking care of not following the same routine twice. The activities include pilates, squats, cardio, or functional training.

Kiara Advani Skin Care Routine

Just like her fitness, Kiara Advani focuses on her skin and maintains a proper skincare routine.

Besides fitness, the Fugly actress takes good care of her skin also. She keeps it simple for her skin care. The actress loves applying ice to her face and believes it minimizes pores and prevents getting dark circles around her eyes. She generally prefers to keep it natural when it comes to her skincare.

Some time ago, it was rumored that she had plastic surgery when her photos in an event surfaced, and soon, the rumor became viral. The irony is that the actress also almost began believing that she had done something to her face. However, she later clarified that the rumors were nothing but fake.
