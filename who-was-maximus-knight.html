Who was Maximus Knight? Remembering the Young Boy Who Died in a Corn Maze Accident

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 19, 2024

On Saturday, October 28, 2023, a horrific accident claimed the life of 13-year-old Maximus Knight, a bright and cheerful boy from Venice, Sevier County, Utah. Maximus was helping his family clean up their corn maze, a popular attraction that brought joy and togetherness to the community. He went missing for a short while, and when his family found him, he had the rope of a tetherball pole wrapped around his neck. He was not breathing and had no pulse.

His family and the emergency responders tried to revive him with CPR, but it was too late. He was rushed to the hospital, where he was pronounced dead a few hours later. The Sevier County Sheriff’s Office said that the incident was believed to be an accident and that there was no evidence of foul play or suspicious activity.

A Bright Star Dimmed Too Soon

Maximus Knight was a beloved son, brother, friend, and student who touched many lives with his infectious energy, warm smile, and youthful spirit. He was the youngest of his family and always tried to impress his older brothers and care for those around him. He loved sports, music, animals, and nature. He had a zest for life and a curiosity for learning new things.

He attended Richfield Middle School, where he was an active and enthusiastic learner. He participated in various clubs and activities, such as chess, robotics, drama, and choir. He had many friends who admired his kindness, generosity, and humor. He was always ready to lend a hand, share a joke, or give a hug.

He also had a passion for helping his family with their corn maze business. He enjoyed designing the maze, setting up the decorations, and welcoming the visitors. He took pride in creating a fun and festive atmosphere for the community. He was especially fond of the tetherball game that he played with his brothers and friends.

Maximus Knight’s Death

As the news of Maximus Knight’s passing spread, the outpouring of love and support from the community has been immense. Schools, local businesses, and households across Richfield have rallied together to offer their condolences and share their memories of the young boy whose life was tragically cut short.

The local school district has brought in grief counselors to help his classmates cope with this sudden loss. A candlelight vigil has also been organized to allow the community to come together and remember Maximus. Many people have left flowers, cards, balloons, and stuffed animals at the corn maze site as a tribute to Maximus.

A GoFundMe account has been created by Maximus’s aunt to help cover funeral and medical expenses. The account has raised over $50,000 in donations from more than 1,000 people in less than a week. The donations have come from people across the state and even from other countries who have heard about Maximus’s story and wanted to show their support.

The account also features many heartfelt messages from people who knew Maximus or were touched by his story. Some of the messages are:

  • “Maximus was such a sweet boy who always had a smile on his face. He will be missed by everyone who knew him.”
  • “I am so sorry for your loss. I never met Maximus but I can tell he was a special boy who brought joy to many people. My thoughts and prayers are with you.”
  • “Maximus was one of my son’s best friends. They played tetherball together every day at recess. He was always so kind and funny. We are heartbroken by this tragedy.”
  • “Maximus was an amazing kid who had so much potential. He was always eager to learn new things and help others. He was a bright star in our school and our community.”

Remembering Maximus Knight

Though Maximus’s time on this earth was cut short, the impact he had on those around him was profound. His zest for life, unwavering positivity, and innate kindness will forever be remembered by those who had the privilege of knowing him.

In times of such devastating loss, we are reminded of the fragility of life and the importance of cherishing our loved ones. While we mourn the loss of young Maximus, we also celebrate the joy and light he brought into the world in his short time with us.

Our deepest condolences go out to Maximus Knight’s family during this unimaginably difficult time. May they find comfort in the love and support of their community and solace in the memories they shared with Maximus.
