Lake Bell children: Meet Ozgood Campbell, Nova Campbell

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 5, 2024

Lake Bell, an American actress, screenwriter, and director, is the mother of Ozgood and Nova Campbell.

Lake Bell and her husband Scott Campbell have two young children, boy Ozgood and daughter Nova. She had both of her children at home, but the birth of her son was especially difficult.

Meet Ozgood Campbell, Nova Campbell

In the late 2000s, the 43-year-old actress dated Colin Farell, her Pride and Glory co-star. The couple dated for several years but never married or had children.

In 2011, after her divorce from Farell, the Boston Legal actress met Scott Campbell, an artist and tattoo artist. The couple announced their engagement in 2012 and married one year later in 2013.

Nova, the famous couple’s first child, was born in late October 2014. In 2017, the couple welcomed their second child, a son named Ozgood.

In 2020, the 43-year-old actress stated that her daughter, Nova, suffers from Epilepsy, a brain abnormalities condition. This news devastated her fans’ and well-wishers’ hearts.

The mother of two is aware of her daughter’s medical situation and is motivated to battle for her health. She has been seen spending quality time with her children.
