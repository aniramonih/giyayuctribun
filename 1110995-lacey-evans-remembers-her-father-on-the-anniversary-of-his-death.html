Lacey Evans Remembers Her Father On The Anniversary Of His Death

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

WWE Superstar Lacey Evans took to social media to remember her father’s passing four years ago. On July 5 back in 2015, Lacey Evans’ father overdosed. Evans said he was ‘broken, addicted and depressed but had so much life left to live.’

Lacey stressed that if anyone knows someone that is struggling with depression or any other issues that it is extremely critical to reach out to them. “Dont ignore the issue no matter how small it may seem. Work on it. Get the help and wake up every day knowing people love you. Life is short and it will get better. The sun will shine again, those good days will return and you are worth it. Dont let it win. Don’t lose. Don’t give up.”

Our thoughts are with Lacey Evans today as well as anyone else struggling through tough times.
