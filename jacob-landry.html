Jacob Landry Bio and Age

Jacob Landry was born on December 27, 1983, in Louisiana, United States. He is the son of famous alligator hunter Troy Landry. His passion for alligator hunting comes from the line of succession of his family, where everyone is involved in it in some way or other. He spent his childhood in the swampy water of Atchafalaya River Basin.

Career

Born into a hunting family, he quickly got caught up in it and became skilled in it. His father and four generations above him also were hunters, shrimpers, lumberjacks, and fishermen. He quickly caught up with the skills and started shining on his career. In the series, ‘Swamp People,’ he made his debut in the year 2010. He became more involved in the show as his father. He is known as ‘Troy’s Deckhand’ in the show.

Furthermore, Jacob is an actor, assistant director, and a former professional boat captain. He appeared in The Queen Latifah Show in the year 2013. He has involved himself in the movie industry. In the 2015 horror movie ‘Paranormal Extremes: Text Messages from the Dead,’ he served as an assistant director and also as a cast member.

The Landry family continuously gets involved in different business aspects to earn money and promote their business. To ensure this, the Landry family launched a website named ‘chootem.com’ to help tourists guide gator hunting. As we know, he is a professional boat captain; he is in charge of his father’s second Gator boat. The family seems to be doing well as the Alligator demand in the market is increasing.

Personal Life

When it comes to personal life, Jacob is very much like his father. They are ruthless and hard while hunting, but Jacob is also a softy when it comes to family. He married Lyndsi Landry on August 7, 2009, one year after their engagement. To grow their family and bring a further sense of happiness and new purpose, they welcomed their first son Ridge Adam Landry in 2011 AD. Four years later, another son Kase was born.
