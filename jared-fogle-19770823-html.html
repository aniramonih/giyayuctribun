Astrology Birth Chart of Jared Fogle (Criminal) 2024

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 19, 2024

Jared Scott Fogle is a former US spokesperson for the Subway restaurant and convicted of sex crimes. After losing significant weight from eating Subway sandwiches, Fogle was appointed spokesperson for the company's advertising campaigns between 2000 and 2015. Born in Indianapolis, Fogle graduated from North Central High and Indiana University in 1995 and 2000 respectively. It caught the attention of Subway and the media when an acquaintance wrote an article in the Indiana Daily Student. about Fogle's 245 lb loss, which he attributed to eating Subway sandwiches. Subway hired Fogle as its spokesperson in 2000, and he appeared in numerous corporate advertisements between 2000 and 2015; He has appeared in Subway advertisements, parodies in an episode of South Park, and has appeared in the Sharknado series, among others.

In the Subway commercial, he was known as Jared the Subway Guy, and he rose to prominence after dropping over 200 pounds on the Subway diet. After pleading guilty to sexual charges in 2015, he was sentenced to 15 years in jail.
