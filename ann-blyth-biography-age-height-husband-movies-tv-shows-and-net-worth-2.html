Who is Ann Blyth? | Ann Blyth Biography and Wiki

Ann Blyth is an American retired actress and singer, mostly cast in musicals in Hollywood, but often active in dramatic roles. Blyth was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Veda in the 1945 Michael Curtiz movie Mildred Pierce.

Blyth is one of the last living stars who worked at the time of the Golden Age of Hollywood. She is a recipient of the Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame which she got in 1960. She is also humanitarian and is part of an inspirational group called “The Christophers.” The group aims to make the world better and also help drug and alcohol addicts along with helping the poor and sick.

Ann Blyth Age and Birthday

Blyth is 91 years old as of 2019. She was born Ann Marie Blyth on August 16th, 1928 in Mount Kisco, New York, United States of America. She celebrates her birthday on August 16th every year.

Ann Blyth Height

Blyth stands at a height of 5 feet 2 inches (1.57 m).

Ann Blyth Education

Blyth attended St. Patrick’s School in Manhattan.

Ann Blyth Family

Blyth was born in Mount Kisco, New York, to Harry Blyth (father) and Nan Lynch Blyth (mother). After her parents separated, she, her mother and sister moved to a walk-up apartment on East 31st Street in New York City, where her mother took in ironing.

Ann Blyth Husband

Blyth was married to Dr. James McNulty (1953-2007). The couple lived together for 54 years before McNulty died at the age of 89 years. The couple received a special commendation from the Pope. Together they are blessed with five children namely, Timothy Patrick (10 June 1954), Maureen Ann (14 December 1955), Kathleen Mary (23 December 1957), Terence Grady (9 December 1960), and Eileen Alana (10 April 1963).
