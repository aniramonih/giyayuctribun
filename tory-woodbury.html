Tory Woodbury is an American football quarterback and wide receiver who was born on 12 July, 1978 in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, United States. He attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he played college football for the Tar Heels. He was drafted by the New York Jets in the fifth round of the 2001 NFL Draft. Woodbury has played for the Jets, the New England Patriots, the San Francisco 49ers, the Arizona Cardinals, and the Carolina Panthers. He has also been a member of the Berlin Thunder of NFL Europe and the Las Vegas Gladiators of the Arena Football League. Woodbury is currently the quarterbacks coach for the Carolina Panthers.
