PHOTO Haylie Duff shares cute baby feet picture on Instagram

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Last week, Haylie Duff became a proud mama when she gave birth to a healthy baby girl. Presumably, the girl is also beautiful, though no one other than the family can say since Haylie hasn’t yet shared full-on baby pictures with anyone. She just got home from the hospital this weekend, though, and already she’s given the world a sneak peek of tiny baby Ryan. Here’s the overhead shot Haylie shared this weekend–one she took while breast-feeding, no less.

Chicken’s favorite spot to sit while I feed Ryan. #BFFs

A photo posted by haylieduff (@haylieduff) on May 16, 2015 at 10:56am PDT

In case you were wondering, Ryan’s full name is Ryan Ava Erhard Rosenberg. And Haylie didn’t wait long to get little Ryan in on the family business, either:  

Ryan is watching her first real girls kitchen episode! Surreal that she was in my belly for filming & so exciting that shes finally here!

— Haylie Duff (@HaylieDuff) May 16, 2015

Real Girls Kitchen airs Saturdays at 1 PM on the Cooking Channel.
