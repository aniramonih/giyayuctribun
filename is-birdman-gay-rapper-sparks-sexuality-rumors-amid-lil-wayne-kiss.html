Is Birdman Gay Rapper Sparks Sexuality Rumors Amid Lil Wayne Kiss

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Birdman is not gay, as he briefly discussed his sexuality in a 2016 interview with Hot97’s Ebro In The Morning hosts. Find out what happened with him.

Bryan Christopher Williams (also known as Baby) is an American rapper, record executive, and entrepreneur known by his stage name Birdman.

Cash Money Records, which he co-founded with his older brother Ronald “Slim” Williams in 1992, is his co-founder and public face.

He debuted in 2002 with his debut album and has since produced four solo studio albums.

Is Birdman Gay?

Birdman is not gay, as he briefly discussed his sexuality in a 2016 interview with Hot97’s Ebro In The Morning hosts.

“Come on, guys, I am a straight gangster, and nobody knows what’s going on with me,” the rapper remarked when asked about the rumors that he was gay.

“I was his father when he didn’t have a father since he was nine years old,” the rapper continued regarding their friendship. And I love him as if he were my own child, and I’d give my life for him just as much as I’d take his.”

What Is Birdman’s Gender?

Talking about Birdmna’s gender, he is male and straight.

It’s also worth noting that Birdman, a father of two daughters, has a long history of dating women.

He has been in an on-again, off-again romance with reality star Toni Braxton for the longest period.

In fact, the couple got engaged in 2019 and intended to marry in 2020.

Rapper Sparks Sexuality Rumors Amid Lil Wayne’ Kiss

Birdman, whose real name is Bryan Christopher Williams, had previously spoken up about his embarrassing encounter with Lil Wayne, but he did so again on the program.

In a recent interview, the 52-year-old Cash Money Records co-founder claimed that he kissed the Sucker for Pain artist out of fear of never seeing him again.

“I always looked at Wayne as my kid, and I always looked at it like, ’cause I was out on the streets, and I felt this would be the last time he ever sees me,” he continued.

Know His Early Life

Bryan Christopher Brooks was born to Johnnie Williams and Gladys Brooks on February 15, 1969, at Charity Hospital in New Orleans.

He took his mother’s surname at first because Williams did not sign the birth certificate.

Brooks and his family used to live on top of his father’s bar when he was a tiny child.

Gladys, Bryan’s mother, became ill and died when he was five years old in 1975.
