Photos: Teyana Taylor Hair Style And Transformation Covered

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Teyana is a multi-talented artist known for her stunning hairstyles that never fail to turn heads. From big and bold to sleek and chic, Teyana Taylor hair game is always on point, inspiring fans to follow in her footsteps.

Teyana Taylor is a multi-talented American artist, born on December 10, 1990, known for her skills as a singer, actress, dancer, and choreographer.

She signed a record deal with Pharrell Williams’ Star Trak Entertainment imprint in 2005, which started her career.

In 2008, she released her debut single “Google Me” and appeared on MTV’s My Super Sweet 16.

Kanye West recognized Taylor’s talent and gave her uncredited guest features on his songs “Dark Fantasy” and “Hell of a Life” from his album My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2010).

Photos: Teyana Taylor Hair Style

Teyana Taylor is an artist known for her bold and daring hairstyles that make a statement.

She often embraces her natural curls and volume, adding layers to boost volume and encourage bounce.

Her medium curly black and purple two-tone hairstyle is a prime example.

This big and bold do is best suited for those with naturally curly hair.

The layers cut around the sides and back balance the body and volume, adding texture and definition to the curls.

Another style that Taylor often sports is a long curly black afro.

This naturally tight curly hairstyle is kept casual with superficial layers cut through the sides and back to boost volume and encourage the bounce to give this big hairstyle a wow factor.

At the 2022 Billboard Music Awards, Taylor wowed everyone with her nostalgic hairstyle that paid homage to Lisa “Left Eye” Lopes’s hairstyle in the famous “No Scrubs” video.

Silver jeweled accessories accentuated her long braids, and her hair was pulled into four asymmetrical buns on each side of her head. 

Teyana Transformation Covered

Teyana Taylor’s weight loss journey has been an inspiration to many.

The singer, songwriter, and actress have openly talked about her struggles with weight and how she transformed her body through a combination of diet and exercise.

She worked with personal trainers and nutritionists to develop a fitness routine that worked for her and changed her diet to support her weight loss goals.

Teyana’s dedication to high-intensity workouts, including cardio and strength training, has been one of the keys to her success.

This talented artist also incorporated dance into her fitness routine, using her background in dance to stay motivated and engaged.

Apart from her physical transformation, Teyana has also focused on her mental and emotional health.

She has talked openly about the importance of self-love and self-acceptance and worked to build a positive relationship with her body and herself.

Her weight loss journey has inspired many fans worldwide to make positive changes in their lives, and she continues to be a role model for those seeking to achieve their health and wellness goals.

Teyana Taylor: Net Worth

Teyana Taylor’s net worth is estimated to be around $5 million, a testament to her success in the fashion, dance, and singing industries.

She has made a name for herself and established herself as a well-respected artist, with multiple talents and skills contributing to her wealth.

In addition to her successful career, Taylor owns a collection of luxury cars, including a Bentley Bentayga, a Porsche Panamera, and a Rolls Royce Ghost.

These cars are just a few examples of her lavish lifestyle and financial success throughout her career.

Despite her success, Taylor has also faced challenges in the music industry, with frustrations with her record label leading her to announce her retirement from music in December 2020.

She later clarified that this decision was motivated by concerns for her mental and emotional health and her children’s well-being.

However, she has since stated that she will not be retiring for good and will continue to pursue her passion for music once she is free from her label’s constraints.
