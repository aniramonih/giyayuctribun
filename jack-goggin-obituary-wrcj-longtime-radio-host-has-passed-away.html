Broadcasting a Love for Music

For decades, Jack graced the local radio stations of Metro Detroit, sharing his boundless enthusiasm for music. In 2005, he became the dynamic host of the long-running Sunday evening show “Film Classics with Jack Goggin.” This weekly, one-hour radio program was dedicated to music composed for motion pictures. The show often featured orchestral pieces, spanning from the early 1930s to the present day, a time when talking pictures emerged, and background scoring took its initial steps.
