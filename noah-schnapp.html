Noah Schnapp's Bio and Early Life

The 170 cm tall Noah Schnapp was born on October 3, 2004, to Canadian-American parents, Mitchell Schnapp and Karine Schnapp. His full name is Noah Cameron Schnapp. He raised in Scarsdale, New York alongside his twin sister, Chloe Schnapp.

After having an interest at age five, he started performing acting roles in the community and school. Later he enrolled at Scarsdale High School and then at Westchester's Star Kidz in an acting program.
