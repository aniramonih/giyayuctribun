Roberto Carnevale Full Overview and Wiki

Musical Journey

Roberto Carnevale, born on June 15, 1966, in Catania, Italy, is a multi-talented musician known for his work as a composer, pianist, and conductor.

Major Achievements and Recognitions

Carnevale has had a distinguished career, earning the Council of Europe award in 1988 and achieving recognition for his compositions.
