Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Katana Chance, formerly known as Kacy Catanzaro, is doing pretty well for herself on WWE’s NXT 2.0 brand. Not to mention she spent years training at WWE Performance Center. She doesn’t usually post sizzling pictures of herself on social media with fans but she did today.
