The Magic Man's Wedding on Mexican Beach

Jon walked down the aisle with his lover Annalise on a Mexican beach. The NFL player and his spouse looked very happy as they shared their happiness with the world via Instagram pictures. Dale, however, has kept her Instagram to a private mode, which makes it difficult to scroll her photographs. 

Jon and his beautiful bride traveled to Mexico with their loved ones. According to Jon's Instagram pictures, the pair took to the ocean before their big day and took some romantic photos. The next day, on June 16, 2017, they arrived barefoot at the ultra-posh Ocean golf course for the ceremony.

Jon and Annalise kissed each other after being pronounced husband and wife. The wedding looked fun as the bride, and the groom walked down the aisle for the reception. After the ceremony, the pair took a trip to Bora Bora for their honeymoon.

Annalise's Wedding Dress on the Trend

Jon arrived at the wedding in his bearded look. At the ceremony, his open-shirted white suit looked perfect to his personality. But as much as Jon looked dapper in his white shirt, his better half, Dale stole the show with her stunning dress.

The fashion experts agree that Dale inclines herself towards the latest trend, and the wedding gown proved the statement. 

Jon Dorenbos is a Father

The NFL Player became a father in May 2019, to a beautiful daughter. Annalise gave birth to their first child in May after two years of their marriage. Dorenbos praised his wife for giving birth to their angel and said she is one hot mama. 

Jon announced the birth of his child via his Instagram account. The couple has named their daughter Amaya Love and even created an Instagram account for her. Only six pictures are uploaded so far in their daughters' accounts @babyamayalove.   

Jon Dorenbos' Second Marriage

Before tying the knot with Annalise, Jon exchanged wedding vows with Julie Dorenbos on April 9, 2010. Nearly 250 guests arrived at St. Augustine Catholic Church to give their blessings to the love birds. 

Julie and Jon met each other for the first time in 2007 during a corporate event where Jon was a motivational speaker. Julie felt inspired by his story and wanted to meet him. Dorenbos heartfully accepted the invitation and went out for dinner.

After dating for three years, they took a big step in their life. The couple traveled to Jamaica for their wedding party, and from there went on their honeymoon to Jade Mountain Resort. Their marital life hit a toll after five years, and the duo part ways with each other.

Jon and Julie both took to Twitter to announce their divorce. While on there, the NFL player also announced he already got into the relationship with his new girlfriend Annalise, with whom he married two years later.
