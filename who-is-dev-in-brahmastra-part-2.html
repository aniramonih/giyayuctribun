Who Is Dev In Brahmastra Part 2? Release Date Revealed...

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 24, 2024

In this blog, we have discussed Who Is Dev In Brahmastra Part 2? Ranveer Singh, Hrithik Roshan, Or Kartik Aryan? Release Date Revealed…

Brahmastra is a trilogy film franchise announced by Indian film director and producer Ayan Mukerji. The first part of the film named “Brahmastra: Part One-Shiva” was released on 9th September 2022 to mixed reviews and has collected more than 200 crores at the Box office.

The first part of the trilogy was studded with some of the finest actors in Indian cinema including actor Ranbir Kapoor in the lead role with actress Alia Bhatt playing his love interest. Others include Amitabh Bachchan, Mouni Roy, and Nagarjuna, amongst others. Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone were seen in cameo roles.

Who Is Dev In Brahmastra Part 2?

Brahmastra Part 2 is all set to thrill the audience after the release of the first part of the trilogy film series. The name of part 2 will be “Brahmastra Part Two: Dev”. As per the first part of the film, Dev is the biological father of the protagonist of the film Shiva, i.e., Ranbir Kapoor.

Now that part 2 of the film is making the rounds, the fans are eager to make which Bollywood actor will play the titular role of Dev in the film. As per the reports, Hrithik Roshan and Ranveer Singh were approached to play the role but it is reported that both of the actors refused to play it, and now Kartik Aryan’s name is also making rounds for the role however, no official confirmation has been made by anyone from the film unit. 

Brahmastra Part 2 Release Date Revealed

Brahmastra Part 2 is set to be released in December 2021. The second part of the trilogy will be based on the character Dev who is actually the biological father of the protagonist of the film Shiva, i.e., Ranbir Kapoor. Dev will play the antagonist in the film.
