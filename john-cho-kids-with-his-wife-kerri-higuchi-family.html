John Cho Kids With His Wife Kerri Higuchi And Family

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 15, 2024

John Cho kids have become the focus of intense speculation and public curiosity, as fans and media outlets eagerly seek details and information regarding the actor’s private family life.

John Cho is a multi-talented personality who has significantly impacted the entertainment industry. 

Not only is he a versatile actor, but he is also a skilled singer who has showcased his talents numerous times. 

However, John Cho’s breakout role in the stoner comedy “Harold & Kumar Go to White Castle” launched him to mainstream success. 

The film was a commercial success that helped to establish Cho as a leading actor in Hollywood. Since then, he has starred in the successful Star Trek reboot and has continued to act in both film and television.

His ability to convincingly play various characters sets John Cho apart from his contemporaries. He has played everything from a stoner to a starship helmsperson and has done so with equal ease.

His talent and versatility have earned him a legion of fans eagerly awaiting his next project. In addition, John Cho is also a role model for Asian Americans. 

He has broken down barriers in Hollywood and proven that diversity is essential in the entertainment industry. 

His contribution to the industry is undeniable, and his future projects are highly anticipated. John Cho is a force to be reckoned with, and his impact on the entertainment industry will be felt for years.

John Cho Kids With His Wife, Kerri Higuchi

John Cho is married to actress Kerri Higuchi, whom he met while they both studied at UC Berkeley. 

They have two children, a son named Miles and a daughter named Emi. Cho has mentioned that he is a hands-on father and enjoys spending time with his family.

Higuchi is also an actress known for her roles in movies such as “The Hangover Part II” and TV shows like “Parks and Recreation.”

Despite being in the entertainment industry, Cho and Higuchi prefer to keep their personal lives private and do not often share details about their family life.

However, they have spoken about the importance of representation in the media, particularly for Asian Americans. 

Cho has expressed his pride in being a role model for Asian American children and hopes his work will help break down barriers in Hollywood.

It is heartening to see a Hollywood couple working towards a positive change in the entertainment industry while raising their family together.

Cho and Higuchi’s children are fortunate to have parents who value diversity and representation in media and are actively working towards making a difference.

John Cho Family and ethnicity Explored

John Cho was born in South Korea and moved to the United States with his family when he was six. 

He comes from a Korean family, with his father being a minister in the church of Christ originally from North Korea and his mother a homemaker. Cho has one younger brother.

Growing up in the United States, Cho has spoken about the importance of his Korean heritage and the unique perspective it has given him. 

He is fluent in Korean and has used his platform to represent the Asian American community.

Cho is a private person regarding his personal life; however, it is known that he is married to actress Kerri Higuchi. They have two children together, a son named Miles and a daughter named Emi. 

Cho has expressed his pride in being a role model for Asian American children and hopes to break down barriers in Hollywood.

It is inspiring to see a successful actor like John Cho staying true to his roots and using his voice to advocate for representation in the media.

His family and background have undoubtedly played a significant role in shaping his values and the work he chooses to pursue.
